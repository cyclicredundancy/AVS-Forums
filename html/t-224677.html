Try <y>, <u> and <spacebar>.
If you don't see all your presets, go to preset/hotkeys and set the director for cycle to <all>.
In Fullscreen mode also <f> for displaying frame rate and there's also a key for turning on title display, but you'll have to search the forums for that one.
