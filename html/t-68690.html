visfractal APE add-in for AVS
http://www.blorp.com/~thor has a package of Winamp related plugins available for download. Included is a fractal APE add-in that's freakin' AWESOME!
The presets that are included are cool, but they get WAY COOL when you start tweaking with the colors. There's three different ways to work the colors: standard 256 color palette, cyclical, and linear gradients.
If you're into AVS and fractals, this package is a must-get! I spent 4 hours last night listening to Steve Vai & Joe Satriani while tweaking with the parameters and goin' "Wow, man!". I could've easily spent more time on it, too.

Psychodelicious, man!
