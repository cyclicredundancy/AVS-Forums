Originally in UnConeD's comment for the original preset
Basically, it animates a skeleton of a star-shape and then creates a polygon mesh of conical tubes around that.

The rubber effect is actually very simple... each spike is made out of several joints which are rotated with a delay that increases with the distance to the center. When the star accelerates, the ends start to lag behind and they swirl around.
The lighting consists of two omni-directional lights that move around.
The actual rendering is done in software too of course: the shape is built out of 1440 gouraud shaded, z-buffered triangles.
