Upgrade
Yeah, it's an option that you do when you install 5.01. The setup wiz detects version 3 and gives you the recommendation to upgrade (and move files from the WA3 dir to the WA dir) rather than leave it as a seperate install and dir.

And you're absolutely right, it's just my problem, so far. But hopefully other people reading this will know what happens when you click the 'recommended' option near the end of the install from of 5.01 if you're sportin' a happy WA3 install. It doesn't necessarily do what it says it will do.

In other words, don't choose upgrade. just leave your old WA3 directory seperate and manually move your presets.
