checkerd - Not that complicated and the edges are kinda ugly.
Color Wall-Bright Mosaic - The colors are awful. It is also very repetitive - try making it morph.
Come Together - Not bad. Try putting a morphing DM on top of that nice effect.
Expanded - Once again I don't like the colors. Don't like the DM that much either. Try to instead use a non-default SSC with some DM's that don't do the same thing over and over again.
Forbidden - Seen similar before and this one isn't that reactive. Plus, that movement gives it some hard edges.
go plese - Very repetitive and static. The weird thing you did with the top half of the background doesn't appeal to me either.
mountain - A somewhat decent DM, but use something that will tile better and look better than the blue "mountain" with the lines in it.
My View of the World - looks terrible on the edges that aren't being cleared. Colors also need to change some more.
Thru the Struggle - Colors are awful. As anyone here will tell you, I don't like the moving particles when used like this. The SSC's are way too random and it shouldn't go all white like that, IMO.

Overall: 2.5. These are very n00bish presets. Learn some more advanced AVS techniques and work with your color choices.
