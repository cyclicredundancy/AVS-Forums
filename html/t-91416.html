Ok no problem mate.:)

I'll start agian.

The pretty pics or visualizations are all forms of plug-ins (additional bits bolted onto winamp).

One of these comes with winamp.

It is called AVS - Advanced Visualization Studio.

TO get to this and other plug-ins right click on winmap and select Visualizations ans then select Select Plug-in.

Then choose the one you want.

Ok you can download additional effects for AVS called presets from the WA or around these forums.


Did you remember what you selected to get you 'two balls' plug-in/preset? If it was AVS then you would have to go into more submenus to select the one you wanted. If you didn't do this it probably wasn't an AVS presets it was probably another plug-in.

Other plugin like at www.winamp.com/plugins/ can be downloaded and installed and selected in the above way.

If it is a plug-in I cannot thing what you mean maybe it is Scope 4d.
