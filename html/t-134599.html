:winamp: Welcome to the Flow AVS presets contest :winamp:

All submissions go here,with a limit of 1 preset per person and 1 update.Please send them in this format : FC-author-name.avs
Here is the FINAL list of rules,if you don`t like it don`t participate and anyone who will be cheating in any way is OUT.

1: 1 superscope
2: 1 dynamic movement
3: unlimited effect lists
4: unlimited number of buffers
5: two trans effects(General APE's allowed,like color map,texer,convolution filter,but no preset specific APEs)
6: one other render object except the superscope
7: any ammount of fadeout, brightness, fast brightness and all the misc except buffer.
9: a small comment on the preset.

You can send the preset until the date of 28 May,that`s 3 weeks from today.
All presets must be related to the title of the contest so anything that aint flowy goes to recycle bin.
As for preset judging i propose one of the mods or all of them or we can get some milkdrop`ers here at the end of the contest.

Have fun and good luck !
