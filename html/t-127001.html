i do hypercubes, cross polytopes, and simplexes - cube, octahedron, and tetrahedron in 3D; octochoron, hexadecachoron, and pentachoron in 4D. cubes are easy - it's just connecting all the points with coordinates (1 or -1, 1 or -1, ...) to any other point with only one difference in the coordinates. cross polytopes are also easy - just connect every point (of which only 1 axis has a coordinate of 1 or -1, the rest are zero) to every other point EXCEPT the one directly axially across from it. simplexes are also easy - every point is connected to every other point, and there's only n+1 points in n dimensions. it gets a lot more complex from there :)
