Multiple Monitor Support?
Hi - I'm having a problem with Multiple Monitors when using Winamp (2.91) AVS.
I have Winamp and the AVS panel running on my secondary monitor and the AVS display fullscreen on the primary monitor.
When I go to change a setting in the AVS control panel, the cursor disappears when I move it over the cp - when I try to click on it, it exits the fullscreen AVS and reverts it back to a window.

Is there a way around this at all?

Jaheckelsafar

9th May 2003 05:28 UTC

Yeah, AVS will not share anything if you have it in fullscreen mode. It will not mutitask. You have to set it to overlay mode inthe display screen. This will set AVS as your background on your primary display.
