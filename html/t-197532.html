it doesn't matter, any acm codec will work in avs. but as pak-9 pointed out already, the end-user will also need the codec. divx is a very bad choice, as it requires -like avs- a lot cpu to decode. you should choose a codec depending on the source-material (similiar to GIF vs JPEG) and a good cpu/size ratio. personally i made good experiences with cinepak, which comes with windows by default.. but i recommend reading yourself into it or ask around on sites like vjforums.com
