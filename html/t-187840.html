Dedications
I had this idea recently and I cant decide if its a good idea or a real stinker. I was thinking about how there's quite a lot of :up:self appreciation:up: in the AVS community... now dont get me wrong, AVS only has a small community so it needs that to a certain extent . Then I got to thinking about dedications, presets people make to honor other artists that have inspired them in some way, its a nice gesture...

Then I though, wouldnt it be interesting to have a 'dedications pack', where people make a couple of AVS presets dedicated to artists who have inspired them or been in some way influential. Obviously some people would get a lot of dedications, but it would still be fun, people could make presets in the style of people they are dedicating to, and include a comment about how they have been helpful.

There are already loads of dedication presets out there, probably enough to make a pack out of :D Anyway, its just an idea, comments?
