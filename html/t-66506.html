PiMPing your plugins
It all depends on how big you want to make your AVS collection. I only recently signed up for the forums, but I've been working with Winamp for about 2 years. I hope my post will be of some use to you.

Most people who make a collection of AVS files they made PiMP their AVS files. PiMP is another method of compressing AVS files. For information on how to PiMP, direct your web browser to http://www.winamp.com/nsdn/pimp/howto.jhtml
This webpage should get you started toward PiMPing your plugins.

Basically, once you PiMP your collection of AVS files, you *should* be able to publish it as soon as you'd like.
