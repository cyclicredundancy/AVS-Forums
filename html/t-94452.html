Transitions AVS Pack - my first batch
I've just finished a modest little package of 8 AVS presets. One of them (Transitions Intro) is inspired by work done by Jaheckelsafar for his Rucksack AVS pack...and yes, I did give him credit, both in the AVS preset itself and in a comment in the AVS code.

The AVS presets are as follows:

1) Transitions Intro (File: Clashman - 01 - Transitions Intro.avs)

2) Back in Black (and Blue and Green!) (File: Clashman - 02 - Back in Black (and Blue and Green!).avs)

3) Clash (File: Clashman - 03 - Clash.avs)

4) Clash Interference Remix (File: Clashman - 05 - Clash Interference RMX.avs)

5) Blittered Blues (File: Clashman - 06 - Blittered Blues.avs)

6) Hyperion (File: Clashman - 08 - Hyperion.avs)

7) Shadeout (File: Clashman - 09 - Shadeout.avs)

8) Spanish Bomb (I worked on this one while listening to "Spanish Bombs" by The Clash, it looked good, so I named it Spanish Bomb) (File: Clashman - 10 - Spanish Bomb.avs)

Also in the ZIP file is a copy of PKZip (PKZIP.EXE), the compression utility I used to create the ZIP file that the presets are in.

Feel free to comment on the quality (or lack thereof! :p ) of the presets, and keep in mind that I basically bumbled around to see what I could create and like.

Enjoy!
