Sorry, but i personally really don't like neon/plasma style-presets. So i won't be able to help you with that color-remixing.

Nevertheless i've got a few tips for 'milkdrop in a pinch':
In my opinion you could make the colors last a bit longer. They merge into each other too fast.
And try to 'fatten' the lines of the scope (use somewaht around 'linesize=8' in the per-frame-box). That way the preset gains a few more shapes.
