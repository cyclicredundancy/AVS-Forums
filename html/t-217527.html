It's not too bad.

You have a good color sense, imo. However, a lot of the presets are redundant, with only mild changes. For example, the lissascope is nice, but there shouldn't be six different presets dedicated to a sine wave. ;)

Anyway, I liked 'Black Sun' (except for the mirror), 'Candy Fields (weak)' for the colors, and 'Light Matrice'. 'Space Beatle' has nice colors, but keep in mind that XOR blending almost always results in something ugly and should be feared. It's also just a little bit too "rapidly changing" for my taste.

Overall, it's, again, not too bad. The good out weighs the bad, so I would say keep it up! :up:
