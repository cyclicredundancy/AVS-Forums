In the chinese, take out the mirror, water, mosaic, and fadeout. These change the final effect by very little(Except maybe the fadeout). Also, try adding a superscope or something to the middle. It isn't complete yet.
Shiny paradise- Nice preset. Could go without the 5-pointed distro movement(doesn't make too big of a difference and adds about 10FPS).
Another disco rmx- Really needs the alpha blending. Turn on the blend mode in the DM and I would recommend using a blend buffer and using a solid colored background. Not a big fan of fireworks either, try changing them to a more dynamic SSC.

Overall rating: 2.5. Add more presets(3 isn't enough) and work on these some more.

The Chinese:
-Make the Interleave blocks smaller
-Get rid of Mosaic and Water
-Make the DDM a regular movement, since it's static (it'd be much faster as a regular movement)
-Make the dynamov move more, this needs to be more dynamic.

Shiny paradise:
-I really don't like chaotic/random dynamovs, maybe it's me, but...gah...get rid of it
-In movement, never use source map unless you can't figure out the inverse movement. It's really ugly, and usually doesn't calculate every pixel.

Another Disco remix:
-The DM isn't El-Vis's (I'm pretty sure), it's UnConeDs. Go look at the original UnConeD movement (try the Alien Chessboard one), and replace the effect list in his preset with the effect list in yours. This uses fog to get rid of the ugly-infinity-chaotic-ugliness.

In general:
-Always put the APEs you're using in your packs. I deleted the 3D APE long ago, cos it was a waste of space IMHO. If you need a 3D object, make your own :)
