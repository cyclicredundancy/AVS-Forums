Submitting
As for publishing your AVSes here, go the plug-ins area and click the "Submit a plug-in" link at the top of the page, below the tabs.

Though I wouldn't recommend submitting small packs of AVS presets when you haven't learnt everything about AVS. It takes 2-3 weeks for something to get published here, so by the time it's out, it's not a good representation of what you can do.
