Without pixel doubling, you'll never get a good framerate at that res. My preset was running about 9 fps without pixel doubling, but around 25 with pixel doubling. P3 1 GHz w/ 128 MB RAM

Other presets:

My slowest one:
w/o pixel doubling, 5.5 fps
w/ pixel doubling, 16 fps

My fastest one:
w/o pixel doubling, 29 fps
w/ pixel doubling, 72 fps (Dot ocsilliscope w/ clear after every frame) :p
