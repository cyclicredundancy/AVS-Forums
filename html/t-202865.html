Am I right in thinking that if a preset doesn't have a Render/Picture setting or Texer II, then that preset isn't dependent on a bitmap?

1 - You're right, if a preset don't have neither picture, picture II or Texer II then it don't rely on any bitmap.

2 - default image in Texer II is embeded into the APE, no external bitmap needed.
