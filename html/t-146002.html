I typed a huge reply filled with tips and suggestions sometime between me pressing 'post a reply' and 'submit' the forums decided to go down.. so i went away and made put my tips into your preset.. if you don't like the aesthetic changes I don't care but I seriously recommend you rearrange your preset into a similar arrangement to mine.. you can create the exact same preset with a lot fewer effect lists and the use of only one buffer instead of two.. the result being a much greater fps.. note that my version draws more stuff than yours and gets 150% the framerate. the only code optimisation i made was a tiny little one in the starfield superscope where i replaced your if(below(something,something),0,1) with an above(something,something) which has basically the same effect, the only tiny difference being when the two somethings are equal. Enjoy!

BTW Shreyas.. your 3D SSC base appears to be an empty preset.

Synching isn't really that hard anyways, you just reverse the rotation order and negative the sines in the rotation matrices, not forgetting to use the camera in SSC by shifting all of the co-ordinates by your camera position. Although occasionally a seemingly random bug can crop up in which case it can usually be 'hacked' around if not fixed.
