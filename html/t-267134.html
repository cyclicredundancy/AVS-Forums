1. Make sure all your presets are in \winamp\plugins\avs\(subfolders\)..

2. In the AVS Editor, go to Settings > Presets/Hotkeys

3. In Preset (sub) directory to use.. , select All

AVS should now shuffle through all your installed presets.

Does that work?
