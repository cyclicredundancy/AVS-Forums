Well...I don't know what your problem is, but I can help you a bit. The 7 or so nested effect lists are not needed if they are all set at ignore/replace. You can reduce them to at most 4, probably only 2. There's no need to have 2 of the same dynamic movement in this case.
And in your main DM, you have a reference to D and R in the per frame section. D and R change during the point section, and are left at (I think) 1 and 1.625*pi at the end of each frame. So you may as well put them in the per point section - the result is actually pretty cool, check it out.
