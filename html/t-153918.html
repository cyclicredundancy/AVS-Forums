AVS presets and Winamp5.
As we are aproaching the release of Winamp5 Gold, we are also approaching the unveiling of a new winamp.com. There will be some changes, among them the AVS presets for Winamp 2 and Winamp3 will be in the same database. (Could be some presets will be in there twice as they have been submitted to both databases at this time.) Over time it would be good if the authors could update the installers of their Winamp3 presets so all will work for Winamp5, and delete the duplicates. Another is the Thumbnail screenshot, this will be 178x75 pixels. Right now the max width is 137, so you can't quite update them yet, but you might consider this for presets you will be publishing in about a month or so.
