AVS is merely a studio for making cool visualization presets. Most of them will be different every time. There is no "end" to the presets. Just record as much as you want to have.

I don't have AVS Grabber installed right now, but I have used it before. I can't tell you exactly what to do, but I have a few pointers. Don't use any sort of compression - just make sure it's set to no compression - it won't always work with compression. Also, make sure you go through ALL of the options. AVS Grabber does work - I used it to create my avatar.

[edit]AVS isn't really that great without music - the real beauty of it comes from its ability to react to music. There are other programs that can make much better visualizations that don't use music - if you want sweet visuals then use one of them.
