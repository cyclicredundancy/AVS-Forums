Anyway back to the topic... I just don't understand what M.A.R. wants to achieve. Posting downrating and negative comments at least has a meaning (though a lame one), but I've never seen someone echo staff reviews before.

The only thing I mind about the AVS reviews is that they are pretty much useless: even the crappiest AVS packs get 2-3 stars. Usually in those cases the only reason it looks good is because AVS is so powerful, not because they spent time creating cool presets. Heck, it's hard to make ugly stuff with it :). I don't want to discourage newbies from joining the community, but having a huge library of homogenous presets doesn't have any purpose. It just makes it more likely for cool presets to get lost in the masses. Not to mention the fact that it encourages others to send in presets of the same level, which causes a snowball effect.
