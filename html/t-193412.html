A really easy way to make an effect list enabled only on the first frame is to enable the evalutation override and just put "enabled=1" in it's init box. Then disable the effect list.

When the preset is loaded, on the first frame it reads "enabled=1" in the init, which turns it on. Then at the second frame it looks in the frame section of the evaluation override, but there's nothing there. Since the enable checkbox isn't marked it just doesn't enable it anymore.
