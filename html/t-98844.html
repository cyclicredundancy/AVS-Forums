Since you seem fairly new to AVS, you're probably still learning how to use it. A lot of your presets resemble each other in a number of ways. A good number of yours are very similar to ones we made as newbies too. That isn't a bad thing---it's just room for improvement.

The psychotic beaming ******d movement limits the variety of your presets greatly. Let me explain: Say you created a preset that is very nice, and you're quite happy with the effect it's creating. Now you want to add a movement. You are deciding which kind, and let's pretend you pck the psychotic beaming ******d. Uh oh! The cool effect is replaced entirely by psychotic beams shooting ******d! And your original cool effect can't be seen. That's why psychotic beaming ******d has its limits.

I actually kind of liked the effect in 70's dance party.

Dark hole was one of your better presets. You used starfields effectively in that preset---but you seem to overuse this render in several other presets. Nice combination of effects in this preset.

Dizcoball gave me quite a surprise---nice effect at the beginning. Could use a little more color, though. I'd like it a lot more if you had slightly smaller squares & more colors in the dot fountains. It will probably lose some of the original effect, however.

Forest fury was one of your better-designed presets. Not very visually appealing IMHO. A little work & it could become what I hope you think is a nice preset.

Try adding some movements to Hellfire. Or try tinkering around with a Roto Blitter. You know hellfire is a type of missile that the Apache helicopters used in Operation Desert Storm?

I believe that Living Fractal looks better with the Mosaic on 50/50 blend. But hey---that's just my opinion---your opinion is much more important.

Marti Gras had a nice movement in it. That preset has some real potential. Try tinkering around with it some more & adding different types of renders, etc.

"Psychodelic" was another fairly nice but simple one.

I think Riding the Nebula would be much nicer with a little more color variety.

I really liked Unnamed. It was (IMO) currently the best-looking preset in the pack. That doesn't mean it's the best-designed one. With some work, some of your other ones could become better than this one

Hope my reviews weren't too harsh :)
