altough your presets show that you had your thoughts while making them, they're still pretty simple. But don't give up in despair: everyone loves their first self-made presets more than anyone else could!

If you seriously intend to make better presets you should quit using only the basic effects. The advanced avs-features (i guess you mean superscope/movement and all other 'codable' effects) are much more versatile. And today you cannot work around some basic coding skills. You can get acquaint to the coding language used in AVS with the help of these tutorials.

and btw: just get used to the crashings. The only two things you can do are: 1.) store your preset you're working on in an extra effectlist, which is disabled as long as you make changes to the effects. 2.) Save as often as possible, just save save save...
