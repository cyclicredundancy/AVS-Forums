www.winehq.com

There really aren`t other options currently.
AVS runs fine under wine with small speed loss for rendering effects and little bigger speed loss for trans effects. There are some extra bugs, like texer 2 doesn`t display colours properly and some minor ones that I can`t recall atm. Maybe newest version of wine fixes them, but i aint going to recompile it (*cough* how about compilation well over 1 hour?)

As for alternative, libvisual guys are currently working on libvisual-avs, it is very far from being finished, but you can keep your eyes open for future releases. There also are many visualisations around, but if you ask me, they all blow (tho' some can even be fun for 2 minutes, like project-M).
