Okay reasons why you will never make APEs in VB/Delphi.

Delphi first:

1.) Delphi is based on Pascal.
2.) Pascal was designed to teach programming by being an easier, slower and generally less powerful version of C.
3.) Delphi is intended for rapid application development (e.g. making crappy database programs)

Net result: Delphi = Shit language for making anything that needs to be fast

Now to explain why VB would be crap for APEs here take the above explaination replacing the word Delphi with VB and Pascal with Basic.
