all in all thats a pretty good first pack, although a few of your presets look dangerously similar to mine (basic colors - primary colours 2, fast and furious - magnetic flux 1) which is cool, great minds think alike. For the most part not to noobish. The prologue and epilogue were good for intro/outro.

Critisms:

1.) NEVER use dot fountain, it always sticks out like a sore thumb and, well, makes your presets look unoriginal.
2.) Try not to use text too much, it tends to work well in intros and if you use some nice scrolly effects or whatever, having it flash up looks cack.

Advice:
1.) Keep on making them, experience is nice
2.) Concentrate on superscope and dynamic movement
3.) Download the work of others, lots of it, rip their code apart if you can
<subtle hint>get my packs (hyperlink at bottom of my post)</subtle hint>
4.) Aim for higher fps, some of those presets where a tad slow
5.) Try to not use effect lists too much, they slow things down
6.) Download my avs packs, I cannot stress this enough, shamelessly plugging my own work is very important to me, it makes me who I am. I check my number of downloads daily, I'm just like that okay.
7.) Shamelessly plug your work and comment on other peoples work whenever you feel you can add something worthwhile.
