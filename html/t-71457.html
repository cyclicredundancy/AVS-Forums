All you need is a graphic-board with TV-out or an external VGA-TV-converter and a video-board.

Connect the TV-out and the soundcard-out to your vid-recorder, camcorder, etc. Switch AVS to fullscreen and start recording.

So you get the AVS with sound to the recorder. Now you need to play it back to your PC via a video-board into some vid-software like adobe-premiere and convert it to any format supported by your vid-card/software.
