1st Collection - comments and suggestions welcome and appreciated.
This is a collection of various presets I have been working on over the past 2 years while at high school :P

Comments on each preset are below along with improvments I would like to make before (maybe) publishing :D

Comments and suggestions VERY welcome, and thanks for taking the time in advance!

(At the moment i could not be bothered putting these comments in each file so hopefully this will do :P)


2Vox - One of my very first presets, looks great with slower music as the white spectrums seem to be surrounding the Center one.

Black and White Blocky Scope Ring - Quite like this one but im wanting to stop the visulisation flowing out of the center after a period of time - probally size of the ring but i cant fix it.

Bump - I like this 3D effect on the vox but i am not sure whether to leave the bump stationary or to move it as it is currently.

Colour Blocky Scope Ring - Variation obviously, one of my favourites.

Colour Snake - Another of my first ones, Only problem being that every so often it leaves one patch of pulsing colour when changing presets.

Colours + Rotating Voxes - Quite like this one as well...

Fading Spectrums - Ill probally go with the first one but im wanting the foreground spectrum to appear fully over the rest rather than slightly see through.

Fuzzy Beating Vox - OK, you probally hate this one and its ver harsh but still :P

Overlapping Scope - LOVE this one, when it moves and the flowing part seems to stand still... LOVE IT

Proper Spectrum - In my searches i could not find a proper spectrum AVS anywhere so here we go - nice and boring but it took ages to find out how to code this, theres probally a much simpler way i missed but oh well :P

Roto Swirl - Simple and Nice

Spinning Rushing Colours - Yes unoriginal name. I was wanting this one to appear as though rushing through a tunel with the colours changing on a beat but still rushing out of the center. Meh didnt turn out but its still cool, however would still like more of a 3D effect on the tunneling.

Static Everywhere - Confusing but looks quite cool at times. Would like to spice this one up a bit, suggestions welcome!

Strange Ring - Another of my favourites, yes the colour map is messy and some ideas on improving this one would be welcome as well.

The Original Spectrum - My first attempt at a true spectrum. This one is very dodgily coded but it seems to work and its a nice change :D.

Water Dots - Would you belive this was my VERY FIRST preset. Loved it then loved it now. I like to think its original but u neva know eh.

Wrapt - Hopefully another original, This is another of my favourites.
