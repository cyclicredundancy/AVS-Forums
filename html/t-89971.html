My favourite was alien. UnconeD's tentacle APE is cool on it's own, but that DDM just made it look cooler (despite being the default one). :)

Overall, work is needed. Rainstorm was kinda nice, but most of your presets are kind of static. Presets are about things reacting to the music, and a plin old osciliscope (?sp) star and regular movement aint gonna cut it. You can do some truly nifty stuff with stock movements and roto-blitters and stuff, but that's just the beginning. I see you tried your hand at making your own movemets in lazer shower, but you didn't catch one thing...

y=52*18 will set the y value for every pixel to 926. Usually what's done with a movement and DMs is relational movement. Something like y=y+.02;. That will shift all the y values up by .02 each frame. Turn on wrap and it'll look like the v-synch is slightly off on an old TV. The point is that you're making y equal to itself plus something. That means that the new value for y depends on the old value for y.
It's how any sort of movement or distortion is done.

Keep it up. Keep learning. Remember, variety is the spice of life.

Oh and PiMP can be found on this website in the Develop part.
