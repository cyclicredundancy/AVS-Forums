Grandchild

14th March 2006 17:06 UTC

are you talking about a)Trans>Texer or b)Render>Texer II?

a)
1 - make a SSC with drawmode = 0 [dots, no lines], or else use a starfield
2 - put trans>texer right behind it, choose a pic and there you have your texered SSC

b)
1 - plug a render>TexerII into you preset
2 - make sure you have a misc>Set render mode on maximum in front of that
3 - code the texer like you would w/ a SSC
4 - everything else can be found through the 'Help' button at the bottom of the TII

so the coolness of the 'shape' your talking about is either dirctly proportional to your M$Paint abilities or your SSC-coding skills.
