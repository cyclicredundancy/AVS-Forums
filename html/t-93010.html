Try this simple method:

beat: q=rand(120)
frame: t=t-0.3*below(q,50)-0.5*above(q,40)*below(q,80)+0.7*above(q,70)

In the example above you will get five different speeds. If q is greater than 70 then the direction will change.

Hope it helps;)
