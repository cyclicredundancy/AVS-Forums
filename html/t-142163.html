14th July 2003 18:21 UTC

If you're using Winamp2.x, check the contents of your Winamp\Plugins\AVS folder. This is where all the presets, APEs and pictures are stored.

For Winamp3, the folder is Winamp3\Wacs\Data\AVS.

If there's nothing there, search your harddrive for files ending with '.avs', that might turn something up.
