MrNudge's Remix Train
All aboard!!

Here's an idea that i'd like to see through and eventually release a pack for.
Basically, I upload a preset I've made. Something simple and what not.
Then someone remixes that preset and sends it back to me, and on to someone else.
That someone else remixes that preset, sends a copy to me and again, to someone else.
This keeps going for as long as it does and eventually, what we end up with is a pack of presets that shows the complete evolution from it's starting form into something probably completely different.

What do you guys think?

I'd have to get a list of people who want to partake in this journey before it departs so we all know who's sending what to who and stuff, but I think it has potential.
