You probably all saw the M&M's commercials with the rippling mass of M&M's...I decided to recreate it in AVS :)

I used most of UnConeD's Seismogrid preset and changed it around a bit...changed the BMP to that of an M&M and it works okay...it still has some dimensional problems though...

The images stay in the same order when they turn..so the one which should be in the back is in the front...it has rough edges....and it looks a bit screwy...

I was wondering if anybody wanted to play around with it and maybe lead me in the right path of how I should go about finishing this preset?
