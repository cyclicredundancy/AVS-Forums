Previews Minipack
I already put this on devart yesterday, but i'll post it here too :)




A minipack with 4 presets from 4 different packs that im currently working on or going to work on the future.

The 4 packs are:

Another Very Small PARTY (aka AVS PARTY 3)
Which is a theme pack of presets somewhat suitable for parties They're fast, they're simple & they have all the colors of the rainbow :p
Preset in this preview: Party Source (new version)

strobotonic III
A theme pack full of Black&White presets, with all the shades of gray in them :)
Preset in this preview: Mind Fragments

Under Influence
A remix pack with remixes of other AVS artist's work, remixed by me :) I'll post a new topic for remixing later ;) If anyone is interested.
Preset in this preview: Tuggummi Remix - Nemoorange - Tri-elemental

Breaking Myself
Kinda like Innovation, but not even close Im trying to break my usual pattern/style of making AVS presets, the future only know will that turn out any good...
Preset in this preview: MTV3 Summer



Oh yeah, you'll need the Colormap , Texer , Convolution & Channelshift APE's for these to work. Included is a texer bitmap used in Party Source
