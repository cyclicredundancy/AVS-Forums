Wallpapers
i know one thing how to put a AVS wallpaper to the desktop! First you go to AVS fullscreen mode and then grab a screenshot by pressing Print Screen button on your keyboard, then go to any major Photo program (ex. Paint Shop Pro) and press CTRL + V (paste) and there's your pic. Then you save the pic in some folder and put it in the desktop!
BTW. 1024x768 res. pictures are much better that 640x480 !!
