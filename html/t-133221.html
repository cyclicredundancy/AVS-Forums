Acid Warp Avsstyle
who remembers acid warp? that old plasma effect, not a visualsation, just a pointless app from about 7 years back.
well if you dont, here is my attempt at recreating it.
my version only goes through 5 modes. the real one used many more,
and also used blending method changes (eg, change beteen additive and xor blending on beat). I couldnt recreate some of the modes and others looked shitty anyway.

so here it is.. lots and lots of experimenting with polar coords.

this is probably a reject from my first pack (/one).. but that depends on response of this thread and how many others presets i get around to completing.
