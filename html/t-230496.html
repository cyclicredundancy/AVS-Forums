hmm...
pak's starfield is definitely better than mine, but do you really want that texer-light-point to stay there in the middle?? it looks a bit odd.
And same with the rotation like ^..^ said.
set "croty" to -reg03 in pak's starfield, that should solve that.
for the color look into the pixel box of the body scopes. there is the lighting [named "col"], and the red, blue and green values which all three take up the "col" value, whereas red is modified by the current distance of the point from the center "d".
To change the colors, just create a global variable for red, blue and green. delete all the body scopes but one. make your changes by modifying red, blue and green and duplicate the scope until you have the desired amount of them. no individual scope setting needed. the position increment is already in the scopes.

GC
