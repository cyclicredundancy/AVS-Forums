Sentence Game
Hey all. I've played this game on a lot of forums, and just felt like opening it here as well. Target of the game is to make a sentence as long as possible, without screwing up the grammar. The good part of it is that nobody can tell where the story will go. Rules are as follows:

- Everyone is allowed to add only one word to this topic.
- Placing a dot . is also allowed. This means that the sentence is closed and a new one can begin.
- Giving a summary of the whole sentence is allowed.
- You may add a word only once. Then you have to wait till another person adds a word to the sentence before you may add one again.
- If topic is dead for two full days, it is allowed to add a word again if the last word before that was made by you as well.
- The one who screws up the entire sentence is an ass-raper.

Hope you all participate. Enjoy! :D
