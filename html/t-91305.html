If you look at the SSC close enough, you'll figure out that the vertical lines are quite impossible (Well, actually takes too much coding) to be removed.

Hmm, I *think* I can help ya a bit...

-If you want to make such tornado, you best bet is to take a spiral (I believe a dense one would be good), make the sides go "up", and somehow point the cam to the right view...
-Easiest way to do so, of course, is to use El-Vis's 3d SSC base code... just remove rotation, use base rotation for cam view, and no movement... The 3d coding, however, slows it down.
-If ya want realism, try to look at a real tornado (Preferably from a educational show. Viewing a tornado in real life can be hazardous to your health.) and see the shape, and try to shape it to the same shape :p But you can always go along with a linear tornado.
