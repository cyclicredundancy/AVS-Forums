Chaotic Lines: Yes, I'll agree that this one has a serious flicker problem. That can be easily tamed, though. Try adding an empty Effect List to the end of the preset, set the input to "50/50" and the output to "Replace". Poof, no more seizure-inducing flickers.

danger sence: It would be better if it had more than just that yellow colour and occasional hint of red. Also, I actually like it more with the text enabled.

Does light escape: I think it's the second-best of the set. The two dot grids combined with that convolution filter gives it a very unique texture.

Dream in colour: Very colourful and very dreamy. I think it's the best of the set.

Evolution never dies: Nice Superscope motion. The clock animation would've been a great touch if it was easier (although only slightly) to see. Try 128 0 128 instead of 64 0 128.

Illuminum squares: That Convolution Filter and Texer don't seem to help much here; lose them. Also, you really need more colour here. You've already got the solution to that in the multiple Timescopes; try making the second one a 50/50 blend, change the number of bands in one of them, then make one "left channel" and the other one "right channel". Suddenly, you've got some subtle but good colour variety!

party through coke: Great texturing effect! Third-best of the set, I say.

searching tunnles: I like the Superscopes (did you program those yourself?), but there's too much black, and the flashing and blitter effects are too distracting.

Security Disco: It has its moments, but most of the time, it just doesn't quite work aesthetically... It looks like you're trying too hard to combine effects that worked well in the other presets. Try removing the Blitter Feedbacks (do you really need three of those?) to simplify it a bit.

White Noise: Yup, that's what it is. Fun to watch for a few seconds, at least.
