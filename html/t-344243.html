gesamtwerk, the complete works 2001-2011
11 years after the release of my first pack halfwayaroundthesun, here is one last pack: gesamtwerk, the complete works 2001-2011. if all went well, this contains all my previous packs (with the exception of IKEA 2), as well as some unfinished projects.

some packs are included in different editions, nothing was edited (not even the embarrassing typos) or removed. thanks to ~skupers and ~da16x i even came across some packs i didn't remember making.

the download includes three files and you can decide how you want to install this pack:

1. grouped by release-year
2. grouped by the type of pack
3. or old-fashioned, altogether in one folder

it is recommended to install pimpbot runtime to install these, otherwise you can always use 7-zip to extract the files.

made in avstria. enjoy!
