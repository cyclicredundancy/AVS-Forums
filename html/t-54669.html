careful...
Transfrmr,
Remember, if you have 2 cards that are both AGP, then you are SOL. You can only choose which one you want to work if one is AGP and one is PCI. If this isn't your case, then you won't be able to switch the cards without doing so physically because motherboards don't have 2 AGP slots (unless your's does...I doubt it).

Anyway, I have a Savage 2000 card with TV-out. A word of warning...I don't know how your card is with TV-out, but mine doesn't allow you to go any higher than 640X400 resolution when it is in TV-out mode. Maybe yours does but mine doesn't. Either it's a shitty card, or....it's a shitty card. I don't know. Just be wary of it.
