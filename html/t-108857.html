I got an idea!
Why don't we all make a group preset, or a group pack of all these group presets. What we do is that, one by one, everybody gets to add ONE ITEM to the preset. Rules are as follows:

1: You must sign in with the date on the preset.
2: The first person chooses what kind of preset is to be made, i.e. if the name of the preset is called "Llama shit" you'd have to make a preset that looks like llama shit...no really llamas do shit.
3: ONE ITEM PER PERSON! AND SIGN THIS ON THE COMMENTS!
4: You may suggest items to be added.
5: Let UnConeD or somebody do the superscopes, we'd like high quality.:) (J/k if you can make the superscope look good with the preset then go ahead.
6: Multiples of the same item is allowed, just make sure that they are exact same (i.e. you can't make 2 yellow fireworks and 2 white fireworks and say they are duplicates)
7: E-mail it to the next person in line, DO NOT POST HERE.

Good idea? Or junk?

If junk, admin feel free to put the padlock on.
