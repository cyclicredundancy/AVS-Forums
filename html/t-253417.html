There are no uninstallers, period.

The best way to organize your presets is by going to (by default):
C:\Program Files\Winamp\Plugins\AVS\
and remove the (sub)folders that you don't want anymore.

Some .bmp files may remain, but finding out which ones are used where is nearly impossible because most artists still don't add their name to their images.

Don't expect to gain a lot of storage space though, AVS presets are usually no larger than 10kb, generally about 5kb per preset
