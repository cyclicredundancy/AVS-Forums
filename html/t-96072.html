Okay---you asked for a review---so I'll give you one.

I see you fixed the bugs in your original psychotic thumpin' preset. I personally liked the first one better, though. It seemed to me it had more appealing color combinations. My taste is simply that...my taste. I personally don't care if you laugh at my suggestions-because they're only suggestions. I think I would like the way it would look if you were to start from the 1st Psychotic Thumpin' & then work from that to get the good color combinations. It's only a suggestion. :)

The movement in PulsingSupernova was nifty, but it was a little too red for my taste. I'm not playing an arcade shooting game! Too much red! See what you can do to add a little more color.

I remixed it slightly---it's just a quick change. I'm sure you can improve it greatly by experimenting adding different renders to get various color combinations.

edit: just saw your comment about color combinations...still might try your hand at renders that have the capability of selecting several different colors to cycle through...that's a good way to get the varying patterns I like best. It works very well if you have a different number of colors on the different objects, because each cycle changes the pattern a little bit.
