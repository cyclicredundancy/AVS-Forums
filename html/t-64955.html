site ring
A while back, I started a ring on bomis.com for Winamp AVS sites (www.bomis.com/rings/winampavs). I've taken sites from search engines and anywhere else I've found, so you (you being the reader) may have a site on there. (email the ring if you have any problems with that).

Anyway, if you chose the AVS preset section in the plugin section, you'd find some AVS packs that have been downloaded from 100 to 10,000 times. There are also 1200 submitted presets and paks.

Unfortunately, there is no easy way to count for multiple downloads (by the same person) or if one person submitted more than one preset. But I'd say the numbers give you a better idea than you just had.
