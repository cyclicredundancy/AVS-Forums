He's implying that your code is horribly inneficient, cos he's a nit-picky noob basher :p

x=x4/(1+z4/dst);
y=y4/(1+z4/dst);

is better as:

myVar=1/(1+z4/dst);
x=x4*myVar;
y=y4*myVar;

Because your doing 2 divisions instead of 4 (divisions are expensive) and the 1+z4 bit isn't recalculated for no reason.
