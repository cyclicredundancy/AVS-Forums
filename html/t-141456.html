How do I make a image of the visualization in order to submit? I also try the thumbnail and it still doesn't work. Can someone please help me?:cry:

anubis2003

8th July 2003 01:17 UTC

Press the Print Screen button on your keyboard(it's up there above insert and delete). This will make a copy of the screen and put it on the clipboard. Then open your favorite picture editing program(MS Paint will work) and go to Edit/Paste. There you go - a nice screenshot.

mikm

8th July 2003 02:12 UTC

Pressing alt-printscreen only captures the active window. It is no big improvement, but it saves time because you don't have to crop the image.

anubis2003

8th July 2003 04:24 UTC

But if you are running AVS fullscreen(which I always do for screen captures), print screen works perfectly.
