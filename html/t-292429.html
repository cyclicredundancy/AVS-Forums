[visbot] Call For Entries
ever wanted to be part of a visbot compilation? this year's summer compilation is currently in the making and for the first time non-members have the chance to be featured. all you need to do is remix any preset from a previous visbot compilation or from the visbot 100 pack. however, to maintain quality, all submissions under go a competitive process. visbot will have a jury to choose a yet undecided number of presets to be on the pack!

try your best, we would love you to play some tricks on our stuff. it's also good opportunity to get some spotlight on the scene.

http://visbot.net/yi-mail.png

send your submissions to the above email adress before june 19th.

for any questions, feel free to post here!

-presets published to these forums or elsewhere will not qualify
-authors have the right to publish their contributions after july 21st, 2008
-on agreement, rejected presets may be published in other packs
-rejected presets can be published freely after the release of the compilation
