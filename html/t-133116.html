Search through the forums for help with 3D. The best way to do it is to figure it out yourself though. Basically, what you do is you come up with equations for x, y, and z, and then use the rotation matrices(use google for mor info) to rotate them, and then "project" the 3D figure onto a 2D plane. There is plenty of info in the avs forums you just got to look for it.

For superscope know-how, try looking through the assorted "Tips and tricks" threads that we have. It really depends on what your looking for, exactly. The best way, IMO, to make AVS is to decide on what you want to make and then figure out how to make it. For example, I want to have a preset with 3D collisions, and I am currently trying to figure out how to do it.
