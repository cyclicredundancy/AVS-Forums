A couple of things:

In your 3D SSC's, shift Z back by sqrt(2r) where R is the radius of the sphere they are rotating in. This ensures that they are projected correctly into the window.
You gave credit to me where credit wasn't exactly due. I got the advice from UnConeD on syncing DMs and SSCs.
