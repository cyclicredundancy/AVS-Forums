1st January 2001 16:25 UTC

1. open Winamp AVS editor
2. go to settings / fullscreen
3. try choosing different video modes (if 32bpp doesn't work, try 16bpp and vice versa), disable page flipping, disable wait for retrace, etc
4. if this doesn't help, update your video card drivers
