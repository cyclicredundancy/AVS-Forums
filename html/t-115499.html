improved Trance preset (incl. documentation for e.g. nested loops)
Trance, Goa, Shakra, Charras, Sun and Moon
===========================================

This preset is quite fast: 10 fps with 640*480 with 1 Ghz. The background looks rather pixelized with lower resolutions.

The background is based on three hand-made Simple renderings using the wafeform. Therefore, it's quite hectic and should be used with Trance music.

The Dynamove shows a long cycle time of 8+ minutes depending on the beat.

Three randomly flying objects (leaves, sun, moon) give the impression to be at a Trance party in Goa. Feel free to copy them if you don't like the background.
