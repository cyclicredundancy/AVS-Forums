resolution-independent texer sizes are definitely needed in this pack and they are easily done: just multiply your sizex/y with the screen height [or -witdh if you prefer] and then multiply by 0.001 or sth to make them smaller again - that's it! i use this:

//frame box
sizer = h*.003;
//pixel box
sizex = [yadayada]*sizer;
sizey = sizex;

many presets look totally different in different resolutions.
