How to stop "auto transitions"?
I notice I can no longer view just one visualization for as long as I want; for some reason, the visualizations keep changing - like a parade of excerpts. How can I stop this and make each visualization stay put until I choose another to watch? Or is this possible at all? Never mind! I figured it out
Figures I'd find out only after giving up and wasting all of your time here by asking for advice. For anyone else who needs to know, you go into WinAmp AVS editor, select "Presets/Hotkeys" and at the bottom there's a tick box with the text "Randomly switch presets." Untick it and you get each visualization for as long as you want without it constantly switching.
