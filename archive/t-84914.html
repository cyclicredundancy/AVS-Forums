A buffer is pretty much a screenshot. With these buffers you can do tings like buffered blends, alpha blending on Dynamic Movement, or just put the picture back onto the AVS window.

To save to a buffer, but in a buffer, make sure 'save to buffer' is selected, then pick to buffer to save to.
