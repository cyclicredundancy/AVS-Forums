I guess AVI's are not used much because they're quite big. Also, why use AVI's when you can create an interactive ever changing video stream with AVS? I've seen some nice presets that use AVI's though.

If you do use AVI's, I suggest you use the DivX 4 codec: it's the codec with the best size/quality now (divx.com). Make sure you include a clear one-click URL for people who don't have the codec.
