Basic beginners persets.
Don't try to fill the screen with a bunch of different scopes, moving particles, stars... it's too much to concentrate on and it just becomes to hectic. Also all your background remain black (which actually is characteristic for nearly all noob's presets) that looks erm.. empty or crappy. try to colorize the background, useing invert, colormaps and others....
Perhaps you should dl some "intermediate" packs (not those over stylish and complex ones made by grandmasters of avs). Just have a look what most people do and you know what i mean. ;)
