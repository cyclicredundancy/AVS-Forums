ssc to fill screen:

frame:
cp=0;n=w+h;

cp=bnot(cp);
x=i*2-1;
y=if(cp,1,-1);
red=blablalblbalalb;
blue=blablalblbalalb;
green=blablalblbalalb;

code your colour.
