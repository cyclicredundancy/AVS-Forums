Hey Manni. Not too bad, although I reckon you'd better get off your high horse... :D

Anyway, here's my rating by preset:

alien brain: Good thing I had the Metaballs 3D APE. :p Good job with this one, although I think the Buffer Saves were unnecessary. 3/5
bluelight tunnel: Unfortunately the d=atan(d) command is very overused, but the fading ring gave a good effect. 2.5/5
BW LCD: Hmm, weird. That's a lot of Timescopes. 2/5
exploding sun: I'll just say this one has potential. 2.5/5
greenlight tunnel: Can't say this was terribly original. 2/5
greeny moves: It would appear that all that Water Bump does is slow down the fps count, but this was nice. 2.5/5
holy: I've seen better. 1.5/5
hypno yellow: I quite liked this one. Could've done more than just rotate though. 3/5
in the eye of the storm: C'mon, you can do better than this. :) 1/5
iris: This was nice, but some colour variety couldn't have hurt. 3/5
moving sun: This was eerie, but enjoyable. 2.5/5
psychotic message: Now this is what I'm talking about! Except you put a space in the dynamove pixel that changed the effect substantially. Still awesome though. 3.5/5
redlight tunnel: It's just not funny anymore. :D 1/5
spider: Erm...the picture was cool! 2/5
starpong: Looks a bit too much like my Fierce Lens Flare. The multiple Blitter Feedbacks just detract from the experience too. 2/5
wheel: Nice pattern you got going there! I wonder who else still uses the original DDM anymore. 3/5
white diamond: Work with this just a bit and you'll get something really good happening, I reckon. 2.5/5

Overall: Some good, some not so good. I don't think you need lots of one effect in one preset. Nor do Buffer Saves at the very top and bottom both on Replace have any bearing on what happens. But you definitely show some potential. My favourite is psychotic message.
