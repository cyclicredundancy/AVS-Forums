Some words to the customization

You may customize your presets by:
-running mode (time; beat; after time but on beat (new mode))
-running time (seconds; beats)
-transition (on; off)
-transition style (you know them for sure)
-transition time (up to 8 seconds in 250 ms steps (so 32 steps at all))
-render old effect (on; off)

You may customize all values or just several ones (the rest remains a general setting)

You will be able to override these custom settings with general ones for live playing. Actually, i am messing with the graphical design of the dual / shared controls and unfortunately i'm pretty stuck.
