AVS has .avi support by default and there is an even better player available (http://goebish.free.fr/vfx/) for it, but both are only capable of handling (very) small files and resolutions.

This is mainly because video files are hardly ever used in AVS presets.

Not saying it's impossible, but I highly doubt that you'll like the final framerate.
