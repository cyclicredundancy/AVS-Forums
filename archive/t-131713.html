I was playing around with some buffer save and effect list's and i came across this nifty little thing. It produces a kind of colour clip effect. You can put one preset in one part and another in the other and from a certain colour in the one later on in the preset the other one will show through. I used a raytraced intersection and some nice colour effects and got this. It has ugly edges but they look like part of the preset for once :)
