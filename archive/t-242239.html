To stop your AVS from transitioning to the another effect, right-click on your AVS screen. Then, open the AVS Editor + Settings + Transitions & untick the checkbox button 'Enable transition on random'. :)

If you want to switch your presets by using your keyboard, easily press 'Y' - backward ... 'u' - forward.
