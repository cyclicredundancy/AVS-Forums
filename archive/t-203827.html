What if I don't want AVS to start every time a new stream starts?
:winamp:
Dear Ma'ams and Sirs,

Every time I start a stream, I have to hit Ctrl-Shift-K to turn off the visualization.

How can I stop AVS from starting every time I start a new stream?

Thanks,
-Neil-

drewbar
4th January 2005 06:23 GMT

Preferences > Plug-ins > Uncheck "Auto execute visualization plug-in on playback"
