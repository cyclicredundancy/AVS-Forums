These are very very noob presets, the kind everyone makes at first. They aren't necessarily bad presets but everyone has seen the same things done over and over again and are sick of seeing them. Don't worry though, we've all made them like that, it's just a case of improving now and you've come to the right place. I'll point you in a few good directions :)

The tips and tricks thread
The tips and tricks for beginners thread
The FAQ
Ateros primer
The search feature for the forums. There are countless threads full of valuable information for you to use and learn.

Welcome to the forums and good luck on your avs journey :)
