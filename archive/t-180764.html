While I am using the AVS visualizations the title of the AVS changes a few times during each song and displays the title of the avs. Is there any way to remove these titles?
TomyLobo

if you dont want avs to switch to a random preset every 10 seconds, here is what you do:
Setting -> Presets/Hotkeys
uncheck "Randomly switch presets"


if you just want to get rid of the titles but want to keep the preset switching, do this:
Setting -> Display
uncheck "Suppress title text in windowed"
