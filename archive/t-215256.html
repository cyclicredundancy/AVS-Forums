Here is the ZIP containing the 50 best presets as voted by the community (the original selection contained 118 presets by the way):
http://www.acko.net/dumpx/Community%20Picks.zip

The whole zip should be extracted into the AVS folder (plugins\avs). So the APE and BMP files go into the main AVS dir, while the presets themselves go into the Community Picks subfolder (plugins\avs\Community Picks).

I didn't include the APEs that are already included with Winamp5.

PS: Everyone in this pack gave permission to be included with Winamp many moons ago, I took care of all that. It's as ready to go into Winamp5 as it was 10 months ago.
