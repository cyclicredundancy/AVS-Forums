Bird - Slow preset. But very nice. The sun is great - good use of an MP.
Hey - Hey back to ya. This is one helluva nice preset. Looks amazing - great beat reaction. The DM is awesome - creates a very nice effect from a very simple SSC. Colors are great. The only problem I have with it are the rough edges(the parts where the DM just cuts off and you just get the straight lines of color).
Kaleidoscope - OK, but I haven't yet seen a kaleidoscope preset I like. Would be a little better if you had a nice 3D DM and this was just a texture or something.
Move v1 - Too many colors piled on top of eachother, and is way too blurry for my tastes. Too much use of the interference effect, as well.
Move v2 - Nice 3D fog-like effect - looks good with this preset. Don't like that you can see the edges of the screen - try to get rid of that somehow(add another Fast Brightness there at the end to see more of what I'm talking about).
