AVS featured in PCFormat magazine
In the March issue of PCFormat there is a half page article on AVS including links to winamp.com and small reviews of eight AVS packs - Whacko AVS, EL-VIS HISTORY, I wanna be Frank Nagel, Tuggummi Innovation, AV Antholology, Why-Eye Full Circle, Danjoe's PIXel and Jheriko J7. Also the coverdisc features the complete works of UnConeD and myself along with three why-eye.org packs, EL-VIS HISTORY, PIXel and Tuggummi Innovation. For those of you who wish to see the article I'll scan it when I get my scanner working... for those with less patience you can order a copy of PCF from www.futurepublishing.co.uk the article can be found on page 134.
