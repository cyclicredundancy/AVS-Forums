1. use only required color-depth (32-bit is overkill, 8-bit should be alright for most color images) for bitmaps and enable rle-compression when saving bitmaps
2. i suggest .7z for compression, it's open-source, supported by many modern archivers and gives even better compression ratio than proprietary winrar
3. if still to big you can upload your files to yousendit.com, sendspace.com and the many others available
