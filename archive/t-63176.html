How to make a avs preset

ok you should have your avs editor open

if not its, right click Winamp and go down to Options , preferences and it will open another window which on the left hand side you will see visualizations ok click it and you will find avs editor


now making a avs is dead easy

say something like Text on screen with alittle effect

you will notice a + and - and 2x

they are basically if you wanted to add a effect or take it away simple.

go to the Trans list and find Text

put your name in it make sure you have both colours selected

ok now
adding Bump which is in render

and say moving particals and thats a simple very simple avs

just play around with it and you will start to get better and one other note, if you like it. don't forget to save it :)
