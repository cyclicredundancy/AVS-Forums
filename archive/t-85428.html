Let's start with the intro.

Focal Point 2 - Make it first. Just copy all the files into a new directory with that one first. It seems to me that AVS organized files by date created.
- It could be made more interesting by adding something that moves to the transparancy of the white.
- I recognized the swirly stuff in the background ;)

Anothe Acid Trip - Nice, but some more changes to the motion wouldn't hurt.

Daisy Chain biPolar Psycle mix - Oww, my eyes.
- Just personal preference, but I like to put mixes after originals.

Daisy Chain - I like this one. I'm a sucker for good use of pastel colours.
- Combine the brightness filters for more speed. (it picked up 2 FPS for me)

Faded Glory Burned Mix - The colour reduction works for this one. I like it better than the original.

Faded Glory - Sometimes it can be a bit bright. It might work better with a darker shade of blue.

Going Blind - I don't know why, but I like it. Usually, I don't like that kind of preset (not much movement). <shrug>

Ruffles - This one seemed kind of plain. It didn't hold my interest.

Starlight Circut - I liked the snowflakes, or whatever thay are supposed to be.
- Liked it better with invert off.

Overall - Nice variety so far.
- Some presets could benefit from some random movements.
- A very bright pack. Too much white for my taste.
