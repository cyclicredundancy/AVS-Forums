Intro - Alright, but screen is very empty and it's nothing spectacular
Addicated v2 - Awesome. This is the way to use moving particles. Great job
Bubblegum - Nice movement. Very smooth.
Cold white light - Nice, but could use something a bit more exciting. Maybe change the starfield to a dot SSC.
Darklight - Not very reactive, but alright for a waterbump preset.
Electro - Don't really like the bump texture, but it's alright
Electro remade - Flashes a bit too much and I don't like the moving particles.
Firestarter - weird shape. Needs some smoother edges too.
Fractal (lemon) - Very unique fractal. Try speeding it up - it looks much better with faster music.
Fractal (slow-mo metallic) - like the effect more with this one.
Free your mind (extra groovy rmx) - Interesting ripple effect, but I don't really like the lines
FrostBeam - Nice light effect. Would look better if there were a few more lights and it cleared the screen a bit more often
Geisha - Nice scope, but not much effect wise. Decent preset.
Gridders - kinda boring. Needs to do a bit more.
Lavalamp - would look a bit better with some better colors.
Nuclear blobs - OK except for the water/blur/bump glitch.
Nuclear blobs v3 - much nicer than the previous one.
Pastel panels - Nice, but the edges between the white and the pastels is a bit messed up
Pastel panels (heavily boosted) - effect isn't as nice, but the speed helps make it difficult to see the edges caused by the gridsize.
Reebok - Seen similar effects before, but it's nice
Skies fall in - very weird colors. Would like it a bit more with white clouds instead of those unrealistic looking flames.
Sky diving - nice smooth preset, except the superscopes are too blocky
Venom - nice movement, but don't like the gray color.
Volcano - nice colors and movement.
Worms (high electricity) - Movement needs to be a bit more dynamic, but it's nice
Geisha remade - Nice. Movement could be more dynamic, but it's very nice
Overall: 4.0 Nice job, and I love your use of MP's in Addicated. Great overall pack.

Shock Value

15th March 2003 18:22 UTC

Intro: I like the shake effect, but needs more. 3/5.
Addicted v2: Very nice. The colors are perfect, and the bump is well used. One of my favs. 4.75/5.
BubbleGum: Also very nice, but I don't like the colors as much. Still very vibrant though. The DM isn't as "fun" as Addicted either. 4.25/5.
Cold White Light RMX: More great colors, but other than that, this one is kind of boring. I suggest using a Superscope instead of a Starfield so you have more control over the dots. 3.5/5.
Darklight: Looks flashy, but it doesn't do much. Could be more. I want to see a version two of this preset. 3.25/5.
Electro: Once again, nice choice of colors. I'd like to see some sound reactivity, though. 4/5.
Electro Remade: I wish you would use Scopes instead of MP's. I like the colors, but the quickness of this preset. Slow it down, and use Scopes for maximum control. 3.75/5.
Firestarter: I'm not sure what this is supposed to be, but it looks kind of cool. Make it do more, though. Also, it could be faster. 3.5/5.
Fractal (Lemon): Neat, but gets boring quickly. Like the colors, though. 3.75/5.
Fractal (Slo-Mo Metallic): I like this more than the lemon version. It's faster and has even better colors. 4/5.
Free Your Mind RMX: I don't like this one much, mostly because it has that every-other-pixel look from the water/blur. 3.25/5.
Frostbeam: I've seen this effect before, but you used it nicely. 3.75/5.
Geisha: Nice usage of Texer. Excellent colors as well. 4.5/5.
Gridders: Stylish, but doesn't do much. Could be more; work on it. 3.5/5.
Lavalamp: Very nice. Just add more sound reactivity. 4.25/5.
Nuclear Blobs: Needs sound reactivity. The shifting bump doesn't cut it. 3.25/5.
Nuclear Blobs v2: Again, needs more reactivity. Looks better than the last one, though. 3.75/5.
Pastel Panels: Excellent! Very nice colors, and great style. Also, it doesn't try to be more than it is. 4.5/5.
Pastel Panels Reworked: Although the changes are minute, this one has its own look and feel from the last one, and is faster. 4.5/5.
Reebok: Excellent colors. Great use of bump as well. This is one of my favorites. 4.75/5.
Skies Fall In: Nice colors, but looks too random. I think a better DM is needed. 3.5/5.
Skydiving: I like this one. Great colors, and a reactive DM. 4.5/5.
Venom: Basicly just like Cold White Light, but with a better DM. I like the shifting colors. 3.75/5.
Volcano: Awesome colors! Like the DM, too. Just get rid of those ugly edges somehow. 4.5/5.
Worms (Hi-Electricity): Very nice. I love the metaballs like effect. Excellent colors and DM. 4.5/5.
Geisha (Remade): Looks great! I just wish the DM was more sound/beat reactive. A cross between the default DM and the last one would be great. 4.5/5.

Overall: Great pack, with amazing colors. Just work on you Dynamic Movements and keep experimenting with 3D. Also, never neglect to make you presets sound or beat reactive. 4.2/5.
