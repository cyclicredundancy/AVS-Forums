Originally posted by Jaheckelsafar
Too bad you can't cycle colours with unique tone.
Yes you can!

Make an effect list
Put a SSC in it
Set it to "dots", n=1, and x=0;y=0;
Put a movement "d=0"
Set effect list to "multiply blend"

The coloring will be filtered somewhat like unique tone, the SSC sets the color, and it doesn't work w/ white =(
