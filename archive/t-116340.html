Trippin galactic snail
-----------------------

I saw a similar poster at a Trance/Goa party. The first version of this preset was published as Gleis 22, the name of the club. In this version the snail shell is drawn smoother and much faster.

If you feel this preset to be too hectic for other music than Trance then
- delete term 0.7*b in DynaMove,
- slow down Fadeout velocity,
- delete the waveform-based frame (first four SuperScopes).

If you enable Trans/Water you'll get psychedelic colors and patterns.

Effect lists are like presets indise a preset. Effects in a preset will not affect things in an effect list (provided they are before the list). Using effect lists you can do simple things like fade things out at diferent speeds, or use them to occlude things like I did in my third atom preset.
