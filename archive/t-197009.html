ctrl+k shortcut to the visualization plugin menu, then select "Advanced Visualization Studio" from the list and hit "Start". Next time you want to open AVS, just double click on the visualizer of the main window.

If for some reason you don't see AVS in the list of Visualization Plugins, you need to reinstall winamp and choose "Advanced Visualization Studio" as one of the installed components. Note: AVS is not included within winamp LITE, you need to download the REGULAR or BUNDLE version.
