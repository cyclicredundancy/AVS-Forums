Okay, I'm pretty sure I know what you mean (I hope)

To get to the plug in screen and turn on AVS, go to the main menu (the little button in the upper left hand corner of Winamp) go to 'visualization', then go to select plug in. This is the plug-in list. On that screen, select the first plug-in in the window. This is the AVS plug-in, and clicking start will bring up the AVS window.

But like Siddhartha said - if you are using Winamp3, then you can bring up AVS by clicking the icon in the thinger.
