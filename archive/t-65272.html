fix
Actually, this is a kind of a hack of a solution.

Choose the blank screen screensaver, then open AVS. In the options, select overlay mode. The color you want should be red:0, blue:0, green:0, and hue:0.

When the screensaver comes up, drawing the screen to black, the AVS should change the black with it's overlay affect.

The problem I can think of is that while in normal useage of the computer, black text (and the like) will be changing colors.
