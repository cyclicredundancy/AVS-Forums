Originally posted by QuadHeliX
I cant get the new pimbot to work with the NSIS installer. you guys have the same problem?

i think i spot the problem.

the latest PimpBot scripts use LZMA as compressor (not bzip2 as before), which is only supported by the latest NSIS version.

you most likely havn't upgraded your NSIS from CVS. to do so, run NSIS\Bin\NSISUpdate.exe and upgrade your NSIS (second or third option, doesn't make much difference).
