Hey Chaz. Sorry it took so long, but I've been pretty damn busy lately. But anyway, let's get to it:

double vision of colours: Somewhat simple, but some very nice effects in places, especially the glowing. Best version is 2.5 movement remix. 3/5
Eye Spy-colorific scopes: Great use of water bumps (albeit a little overkill), and the gradient colour SSC is cool as well. But you might want to find a better use for it. 3/5
kaleidascopophobia: Pretty harsh on the eyes. Liked the movement though. 2.5/5
Escape from coloristiconica: Damn you use big words. :) This one's nice as well. Turning off some of the fireworks improved on the overall experience. 2.5/5
Disco DaFunk: That SSC is a mighty fine piece of work. The occasional flashes of red aren't quite at home. Best version is tornado. 3/5
drunken smoking flares: Another good application for the water bump. Colours are somewhat bland, but overall a great preset. 3.5/5
ghosts in another dimension: Hmm, not very ghosty, but okay for what it is. 2.5/5
chaz parkinsophobic: I thought it looked like something that justin would make. The spattery effect is just lovely, although a bit hard on the eyes. 3/5
Shockwave Damage Show: This is pretty bland, but keep working with it. 2/5
4th of July: A good example of "less is more". Best version is smoke & comets. 3/5
robomatic colortile scopes: I have no idea what people see in the Spiral Graph SSC, but it's just me. Another nice movement effect as well. 2.5/5
mirrors mirrors everywhere: A bit too psychedelic, but again, the movement saves it. I didn't know you could set RGB values in frame, either. 3/5
psycho color well: It looks a bit too much like previous presets to me. 2/5
Artificial Intelligence Generator: This is more like it, but could use some work. Great combination of the mirror and blitter feedback. 3/5
Journey paranoya color: This seems to belong to the family of the last few presets, but still takes its own direction. 2.5/5
rotating color meltaway superscope: Wow, this is awesome! The melting effect is fabulous. Tone down the colour of the SSC and you've got one hell of a relaxation preset. 3.5/5
random color generator: Ahh, my eyes! You might want to play with this a little. Turn the output on the list to 50/50, maybe. 2/5
color waterfall: That's the most psychedelic thing I've ever seen! Still good, though. Try making it more dynamic. 2.5/5
color blobulae mind beaters: This is quite nice to look at. Sure beats having to use stacks of starfields. 3/5
Sauron's Fiery Eye: Ah, the legendary LOTR eye. It's definitely improved since 2.5. Adding a unique tone might help to make it more fiery. 3.5/5

The verdict: A damn good start to an AVS career (for want of a better word). Some presets were a bit too similar, but you made up for this with some great coding and use of some "underground" effects like the water bumps. Keep working at it, you're doing a mighty fine job. My favourite is drunken smoking flares.
