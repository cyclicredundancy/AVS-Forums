I just did a quick hack of AD to enable saving and loading of the construction. To open it just put "globmgr.ape" in the avs folder, and the other file in any subfolder of the avs folder.
To save: double tab n.
To load: double tab m.

It's just a quick hack that dumps the contents of the regs and of the gmegabuf 0-100000, thus saving can be rather slow. At first I considered changing the format of the builing, because it's currently quite ineffective, but because it's such a big avs file I really couldn't be bothered with it.
