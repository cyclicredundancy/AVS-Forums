Cool Thing to do with AVS overlay and ICQ(kinda advanced)
y'all know that avs overlay works with a specific colour
so i decided to find that colour,, simple task, and i started putting lots of things of windows that colour
put your icq that colour! so you can see your whole monitor's worth of the wonderful world of AVS

and in controle panel /display/apperance/item-3d objets, put it that colour,, it's funky

the *default* colour of the overlay is(in 32 bit):
hue221 red 31
sat240 green 0
lum 15 blue 15

try it!
it's cool lookin!
