Summary:
Videocards are 'stupid machines' that can only do certain things very fast. Milkdrop is more limited than AVS, so it can work on a video card. Doing AVS on a videocard is becoming possible now that videocards have pixel-shaders, but few people have such cards. It would also require a redesign from the ground up.

Except for a few fanatics, the AVS and Milkdrop communities are quite friendly to eachother ;).
