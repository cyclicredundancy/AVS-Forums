Highest Rated Presets from Deskmod
Since Deskmod bit the dust, and then resurrected itself, my previous Preset uploads to Deskmod have been lost. I just uploaded a zip file of 58 presets, which represent my former highest rated presets. These are presets that received a 90+ score from Deskmod. Most all of these represent the early developement of AVS, and do no use a lot of the new effects. But they do represent some pioneering and historical value in the evolution of AVS. Hope you enjoy the oldies. :)
