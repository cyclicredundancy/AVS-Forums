Originally posted by Cyth
and i know that AVS uses the CPU instead of the vid card so re-installing my video drivers wont help.

Actually in this case it is your best bet.

Overlay mode uses a hardware feature on graphics cards.

For future reference this kind of "dirt" on the screen is the fault of the graphics card 90% of the time. Similar junk gets rendered if you overclock too far or if the driver is unstable.
