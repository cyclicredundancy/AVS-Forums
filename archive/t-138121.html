Desaturization filter kinda thingey.
Don't know where to put this, honestly...tips/tricks, wishlist, presets...basically this is a preset that demonstrates a desaturation filter I made a couple days ago. It tones a pixel towards the average of it's three color channels. No, it is NOT the same as a utone (which uses the maximum of the three color channels), or a brightness-filter colormap (uses the log (?) of the average). The only problem is it's SLOW AS HELL due to all the effect lists and adjustable blends :p
So this is a poke at UnConeD to add an average keying to Colormap, instead of that Brightness key (which, as far as I can tell, is rather pointless in the first place because it seems to ignore a large portion from anything between light gray to white). Hence I don't know where to put it :D

And that DM rocks, I don't care what you say, it just does. ;)
