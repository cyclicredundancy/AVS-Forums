Raz, I like this DM a lot more. Good job speeding it up. However, It moves a bit too fast. It's kinda blinding when it moves that fast. At least have it accelerate smoothly. Also, there are still some weird lines that come up. I assume because of one of your DM's.
To keep it from fading to a solid color multiply the alpha by some number(experiment to find what you like). It is fading out because the "fog" is really close and can cover up most of the screen at times. By increasing alpha, the fog moves further away and the screen won't go black.
