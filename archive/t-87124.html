GIF
Oh by the way, GIF is always 8-bit. The reason paint messes it up is probably because it uses a standard color palette instead of a specially calculated one suited for your image.
A better replacement format is PNG: it supports any color-depth, has better compression, supports alpha-channel transparency (though most browsers don't render that) and is patent-free.

Also, you might want to try http://astalavista.box.sk/. It's a search engine to locate um... 'utilities' to help you with that evaluation-version problem.
