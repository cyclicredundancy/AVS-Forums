I'm not going to do a big in depth review as I don't have the time right now, but I'll give you a couple of points.

1. Begin the name of your presets with a number so that they load in a specific order on my computer - the intro should be first, etc.
2. A couple of the presets are kinda n00bish, but it looks as though you are progressing out of that.
3. Several are way too bright. Tone the colors down some - nothing is worse than a screen that just goes completely white for 5 seconds or so.
4. Some of your DMs are way too jumpy and fast. Slow them down some. Along with bright colors this really begins to hurt the eyes.

Overall Score: 3.5. Good, but not terrific. Keep up the good work though - you're getting better.
