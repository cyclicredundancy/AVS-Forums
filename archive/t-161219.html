A solution to one of the wishlist items, and a wish.
The solution:

Drawing Options
  • The endpoints of thick diagonal lines are always horizontal or vertical. Having an option for correct endpoints would be very handy for advanced scopes.
This can be done with Texer, by SSCing a 1 pixel wide line, then expanding it with a circular Texer on maxblend.

Or, if you want to work on a background, use Texer II.


The wish:

The ability to use a buffer (or region of a buffer) as a Texer bitmap.
