How do I release these packs so rapidly?

Well whenever I have an idea for an AVS preset I make it when I next sit down to the computer, if its a complex idea I'll work it out then force myself to make it. Even when I don't enjoy making a particular preset I force myself through it just to see the end result. Sometimes I'll sit down and make 3 presets in a row (I did this with triacontadihedron, random access and power) and I tend to spend little more than an hour on most presets... sometimes if one bugs me a lot then i'll take hours over it but mostly my presets are made quite quickly and not by random experimentation.
