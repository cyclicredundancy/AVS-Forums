The 'Advanced Visualization Studio' comes with the standard and full version of Winamp. You can download additional presets at winamp.com. Just install the presets. Then go to the Preferences and start AVS, you can choose the different presets by rightclicking on the AVS window.

I luv AVS :)
Just open the AVS (Ctrl+Shift+K)

Right click on the window that opens up. There, there should be a series of presets that you can choose from.

Also, to make presets, left-click on the screen and another window will pop up. Use the + button in that new window to add render effects and others. This takes a while to get it right, but if you work at it, you can make some really good ones.
