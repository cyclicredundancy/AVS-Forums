um...do you totally want to remove it forever

delete vis_avs.dll(usually found c:\program files\winamp\plug-ins)

you do you just want it to stop loading at startup

preferences (ctrl+p)-->plug-ins-->uncheck auto-execute vis plug-ins at startup-->close

or just so winamp doest load it

rename vis_avs.dll to vis_avs.dll.off (usually found c:\program files\winamp\plug-ins)
