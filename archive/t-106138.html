Nope. The only thing your video card does is receive the image from system memory and display it, i.e. nothing more than it is doing regularly in Windows.

All the important work is done by the CPU... this can easily be proven by looking at the APE SDK: the image is an array of bytes in system ram.

A faster/newer video card will make little difference, unless the previous one was really old and had crappy drivers.

Because of all the attention video cards have been getting recently, lots of people seem to consider themselves self-proclaimed graphics experts :P.
