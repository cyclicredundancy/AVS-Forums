at one point, the guys at nullsoft changed the way to skin the avs window. running an old version of winamp, it was the avs.bmp file. in winamp 5 (and the last versions of winamp 2) this was changed, it is the file gen.bmp, which is used by the avs window and the medialibrary. however, due to the different layouts of these two files, you can't simply rename the old avs.bmp to gen.bmp.

you have to update these skins yourself or hope for an updated version from the author.
