Fired Spectrum
Thanks to UnConeD, idea and some code for fire and smoke effects was taken from his Whacko AVS II - Intro II.

Recommended modes for proper view:

1. Windowed mode
540x200 with pixels doubling turned ON
or
270x100 with pixels doubling turned OFF.

2. Fullscreen mode 800x600 32bpp with pixels doubling turned OFF and with display rendering zone set to 50%

3. Overlay mode with one of windowed modes.

Unzip to avs presets folder and enjoy. Works with WA3 and WA2 (tested with 2.91).
