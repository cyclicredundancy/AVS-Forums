First of all, BE PATIENT. Those kinds of 'please help!!!' posts are really annoying, and won't get you anywhere.
Don't use so much blur, and don't use so much colorfade. Use higher-resolution DMs when you use them; and don't make/use ugly random/chaotic DMs. Use brigher (as in luminosity/saturation) colors. Never remix a preset without including the original name and the author's name in the remixed preset name - e.g. "Rush (Endless Pursuit rmx) (Zen-X remixed)"

Other than the fact that you need to learn what you're doing, your presets are original and, with some tweaking, watchable. Good luck
