I typed a huge reply filled with tips and suggestions sometime between me pressing 'post a reply' and 'submit' the forums decided to go down.. so i went away and made put my tips into your preset.. if you don't like the aesthetic changes I don't care but I seriously recommend you rearrange your preset into a similar arrangement to mine.. you can create the exact same preset with a lot fewer effect lists and the use of only one buffer instead of two.. the result being a much greater fps.. note that my version draws more stuff than yours and gets 150% the framerate. the only code optimisation i made was a tiny little one in the starfield superscope where i replaced your if(below(something,something),0,1) with an above(something,something) which has basically the same effect, the only tiny difference being when the two somethings are equal.
