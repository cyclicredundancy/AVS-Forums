PAK-9

People release remix packs fairly often, so no there is no problem with it.

For naming it is conventional to use the original preset name followed by the name of the remix and who it is by in brackets. Most people request that you include the original preset comment with the remix and it is generally polite to do so.

Those are just guidelines and generally accepted practice however, you may want to contact the preset authors directly if you are particularily concerned.
