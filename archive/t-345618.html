read this (http://visbot.deviantart.com/journal/The-State-of-AVS-218486857) about how to avoid compatibility issues. i'd suggest you upgrade (http://avs.visbot.net/) to avs 2.81, as 2.5 is super-old.

if the problem persists, you might have to delete/move the last active preset. that's the vis_avs.dat in your winamp plugins folder. once deleted/moved, avs should launch with a blank preset.
