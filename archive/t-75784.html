init: a=0
frame: a=a+.03
beat:
pixel: x=x+.1*sin(a); y=y+.1*sin(a)

There's a simple one. Effect best seen with the Ring or Moving Particle renders. Be sure to check "Rectangular coordinates."

Want it to change speed on beat? Try this:

init: a=0; c=.03
frame: a=a+c
beat: c=rand(10)/100
pixel: x=x+.1*sin(a); y=y+.1*sin(a)
