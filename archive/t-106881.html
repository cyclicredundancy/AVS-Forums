You can get AVS to ignore it for one frame like this:

Effect list (replace/replace)
.Custom BPM (reverse)
.Effect list (not enabled, onbeat set to 1 frames) (replace/replace)
..<Effect>

You can alpha-blend the effect out with a DM, but how you do that depends on what you're doing. It doesn't stop the effect from being processed, but it stops it from working.
