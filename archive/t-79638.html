Well, I won't do 'em all, but here goes.

individual

* animatext - Good visuals. Creative use of text as a filter. Would benefit from better beat detection, but what can you do?
* crude painting - Good choice of colours. The backgroud really helps make this one. It brings the whole window in on the action and gives the colours a nice irregular fade.
* cyclone - Would benefit from some changes in direction of movement. A bit of red or yellow could do wonders.
* filters2 - Nice, but more movement in the background would make it more interesting.
* final frontier - Not my bag. I never really liked that kind of preset.
* interferenz - I never really liked the interference filter.
* kaleidoscope - Too much white. It's hard on my eyes at 6 AM after crashing on the livingroom sofa.
* kaleidoscope 2 - I always found this type of preset nice and colourful but kind of boring. I prefer movement as a whole. (see moving kaleidoscope in my preset pack)
* other kaleidoscopes - too slow on my comp.
* marble floor - Again, the white thing.
* neon lights - Liked it, don't really know why. It just came together for me.
* nowhere to be found - I'd prefer a gradually changing superscope and a less than instantaneous switch of rotation direction. If it had that, this would be a winner for me.
* our planet- I liked it except for the unfortunate bleeding of the edges that movements produce. Aside from that it's good.
* pillaroid - Holy crap! that's a lot of DMs. Combine some of them and should speed things up. It would be nice if the movement reacted to the music.
* SCSVwBD - Reminds me of MOD players. <sniff> Those were the glory days. <snicker>
* sightseeing - Liked that one.
* stargazing - Smoth tansitions on the movment would be good.
* welcome to the math class - Perhaps if my math class were this interesting I'd have stayed awake :)
* wheel of life - I wouldn't mind a definate sence of something on top. I find it a little to blended.
* wish upon the stars - I dunno, this one just didn't click
* kaleidoscope 3 - I like smooth transitions. Maybe it's just the type of person I am, but I like flowy stuff.
* cosmic blanket - Smoothness again
* galacticon - I liked it better with the first unique tone turned off... that whold brighness thing again (though it's not nearly so bad now that the sun is up). You made the right decision about adding the cos in the DM.
* fiery aftermath - Ahh... reminders of the demo scene. I liked it.
* waterscope - Nice and relaxing.
* pure Insanity - Painful to watch (ouch my eyes)
* plasma - Heh heh... looks like a spider getting electocuted. I did like it though. The negatie bars moving around gave it an egde. Now if only they could move independantly and cancel eachother out.
* mixaroo coloradoo - Bliss.

overall

* very bright
* not too shabby
* lots of presets give it variety, but be careful not to let quantity replace quality
* 3 / 5

misc comments

* The files are listed in the order you copy them into the directory. If you want your intro first, copy it first.
* You like bright things.
