My global-var getosc hack is not what you imagine it to be. It allows you to export values from one superscope into other components, but only through a painful process and with very limited precision. The superscope in question can NOT draw anything and has to have all its code in its perframe section.

It's only useful for extreme presets (and I mean really extreme, not just a 3D DM) where global vars are simply necessary and synching is not an option.

Real global vars will require an AVS code update, no APE can fix that.
