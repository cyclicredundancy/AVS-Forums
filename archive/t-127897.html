Ok, i'll try to do a preset by preset review :)

Arcane - 2.0 - Nice, quite peaceful and smooth. Good effect with the mosaic too.

Deviant's Revenge - All of them pretty much lack all beat detection & music reaction and they just look good, except the one with only the scope, that looks kinda empty.

Diego 2.0 - Not much to say about this. Looks pretty basic and i don't like the channel shift.

Diego - Cel Remix - Check above.

Phazer Speaker - Add a fadeout.

Felluca - Nice job on re-creating duo's preset, but it looks pretty dull and doesn't slide&swing like the original.

Fortification - Justin's fireworks Kaliadascope mk 2 again?

In Secret Love we drown - Nice dm & pattern, but the pattern could look more continous and it could have some colors to spice it up.

Dragonmatrix - Decent remix. Every other line blend makes it look a bit nicer.

Machinaeon - Another board preset. The idea is beaten to death you know? Of course board presets are cool sometimes if the pattern is great, but the pattern in this one looks ugly to tell the truth.

Nightstar 2.0 - Again the fyrewurx & water like in the last preset (above). Static & unreactive. Not good.

Nocturnal Pulse - Moves, Good beat detection, good pattern. You did a good job. On of the better presets of the pack.

Shrine of Spirituality - Dull & unreactive. Really basic.

Stargazer's Dream - Again moving particles & effect lists. And what is that dynamove doing there? It seems to have no effect on the preset.

Superspyro - Looks nice, but unreactive.

Taloria - A word of advice: Using d,r,x,y in any other section than pixel has no effect whatsoever. If you're not making the last dynamove dynamic, then why to use it in the first place? A regular movement is faster & more accurate.

Trammel - The last dynamove is pretty classic. The dm above it could use some randomized beat detection.

Ultimate Trap - Again lacking in music reaction. The Spyro one looks nice tho.

Vertigo - Can't really say i like them.

White Dragon - Nice colors and glow. Simple yet nice and music reactive scope. Blending effects are good too. Best preset in the pack and the one that made me to write this whole review :)


Still pretty basic stuff you got here, but i see some improving in the presets. You could need some more learning in how to make presets music/beat reactive and some coding since sometimes your code made no sense or had no effect.

Keep trying :up:
