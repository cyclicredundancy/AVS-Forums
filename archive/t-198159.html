Also, a note about Spiradelic 3: if you don't get any colors showing up, listen to a louder song :) . Actually, there's a fix for this, go into the superscope and find the line that says:

ce = max(.04, getspec(.5,1,0));
and change it to:
ce = max(.2, getspec(.5,1,0));

This will make the color show up even during the quiet sections.
