1. open the avs plugin
2. double-click inside the avs window to open the editor
3. click on Main in the editon and active Clear every frame
4. click on the plus and select Render\Picture (or Render\Picture II) or AVI
5. click on the effect and load a bitmap/avi

to install the Picture II APE, download it and place it in the Winamp\Plugins\avs directory. there's also an APE that captures images from a connected webcam.
