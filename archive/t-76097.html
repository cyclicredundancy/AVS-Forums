my opinion
I think the colors in your visualizations need work. Most seem to be random, bright, and scattered. Not that it's necessarily bad thing; it looks good in chaz 6.5.5 and even more so with underwater fireworks (which I believe to be your best-looking one). But, I'd recommend trying to change the render mode to 50/50 blend or to use less color clips.

Also, I'd reccomened making sure of the order of your renders and trans effects in the list. Like in "c much better laser-like effect 3" (odd name, like most of yours), the blur effect is near the top, but if you move it to the bottom, it blurs the superscope, as to make it look less like pixels, but without being slower.

I'd like to see your bright ideas (haha, I'm so punny), but the need work at the moment.
