as you might know already, avs also works with old versions of foobar2000 (http://www.oldversion.com/program.php?n=foobar) (0.8.x only). all you need is the baconwrap plugin. for detailed instructions, continue reading here (https://www.msu.edu/~singergr/installation_nowinamp.html).

however, texer images will not be displayed when using avs with foobar2000. have a look at this list (https://www.msu.edu/~singergr/list.html) for more compatibility issues.

-baconwrap (https://www.msu.edu/~singergr/bacon.html)
