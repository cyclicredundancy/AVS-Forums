But seriously, do you really want something as ugly as wmp visuals? I can make any wmp visual that I've seen so far within 5 minutes in AVS and make it look better in 6.
Take a look at this (http://www.winamp.com/plugins/details.php?id=155866) or this (http://www.winamp.com/plugins/details.php?id=159946) for what AVS can do. I hope it'll convince you to step away from wmp visuals.
I know that I sound like I'm promoting AVS here, but for me it's more that I absolutely hate the WMP visuals. They're just that ugly. :down:

You may also want to take a look at milkdrop (http://forums.winamp.com/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=81) if you like wmp-like visuals.
