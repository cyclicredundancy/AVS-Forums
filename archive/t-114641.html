All right Zev, here's my detailed review:

Intro: Excellent use of superscopes, and I liked the meter as well. Personally I thought it looked better without the Invert, but anyway. 3.5/5
Metablobs: Wow, what a great effect! This definitely rivals the Metaballs APE. I had terrible slowdown (~ 3fps) but that's gotta be expected I guess. 4/5
MetablobsCartoon: Just looks the same to me, although the celshading effect isn't bad. 3.5/5
MetablobsRadioactive: Alright, it's not funny anymore. :) I think this one's the best out of the Blobs bunch. 4/5
SwirlingVortex: Excellent coding and a nice overall effect. But it's a bit too dark, and some extra dynamics couldn't have hurt. 3.5/5
Realtime Raytracer: GM Chrysler! You can really code. I would've put this to a better use, but the fantastic techwork more than makes up for it. 4.5/5
Fireworks: Another great example of coding. I 'unno, I'm really lost for words about this. 4/5
SingleCellAlien: A bit similar to the Blobs presets, but the overall effect is fantastic. 3.5/5
SuperEllipsoid: The colourcoding in the SSC here is phenomenal, as is the occasional seperating of the two sections. Still, the preset is a bit too static. 4/5
Milkdrop2: Yet another example of your technical prowess and a nice flavour of aesthetic beauty. The transitions are spot-on. 4/5
SuperEllipsoid Dark: Looks good with the Invert, but it's almost identical to the original. 3.5/5
FloatingBlocks: Coupled with the gradient BG, these objects give a great effect. A little blocky though (excuse the horrible pun). 4/5
DigitalGrey: Great swirly movements, and the mosaic is a very nice touch. 4/5
WaterDrop: The plane jerks a bit, but it's still one of the finest examples of coding I've seen. 4/5
Desert Sunset: A very original idea, and it's pulled off almost perfectly. I was going to suggest that the canyon be assymetrical, but I see it's unavoidable. 4.5/5

The verdict: This is probably the best AVS pack I've had the pleasure of reviewing. Your coding abilities are right up there with UnConeD and EL-VIS, and your preset ideas are very original. Just don't repeat them too much! :)

Aesthetics: :) :) :) :( :( 1/2
Abilities: :) :) :) :) :( 1/2
Originality: :) :) :) :( :( 1/2
Overall: :) :) :) :) :(
