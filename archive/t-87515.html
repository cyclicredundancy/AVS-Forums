#1 : Start AVS.
#2 : Double click on the window to open the configuration box.
#3 : Click on Settings
#4 : Click on Presets/Hotkeys
#5 : Look down there. Uncheck the box that says "Randomly Switch Presets"

Otherwise, do NOT press spacebar when the AVS window is active (It's ok to do so when the configuration box is active. Active, not passive.) - It changes AVS to another preset.
