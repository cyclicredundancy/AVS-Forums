out of this pack, the box presets really stood out...but they both need to be more dynamic. your syncing consists of a static on-beat setting (bad) - use something like:
(getosc(0.47,0,0)*8753%100)/500-0.1
this takes an arbitrary oscilloscope position and permutates it to the point of fair randomness :) if you don't know what any of that means, study up on the modulus operator (%).

the vecto preset was also very good, but it too needed to be a lot more dynamic. overall all of your 3d shapes need to be more dynamic - you've got a good handle on modeling them, now you need to polish them off and make them look good :)

hope i've been a help,
