Okay, the first blank, where it says "Name," is where you put the title of your pack, i.e. "philgump's Pack" (Letters & numbers only in this blank.)

The seventh blank, where it says "Component," is where you put the filename, i.e. "C:\Program Files\MakePimp\philavs.exe" (Use the browse button next to the blank.)
