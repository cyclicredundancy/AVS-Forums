why do the AVS Presets Keep changing?!, how can I stop it and make it use one preset?

DEckaRD
21st December 2000 17:49 GMT

Start AVS and doubleclick on the running preset. A configuration window appears. Goto Stettings - Presets/Hotkeys and uncheck the box 'Randomly switch presets'.
