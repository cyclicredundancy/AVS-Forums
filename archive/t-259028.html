Since AVSgrabber is not officially supported here (or anywhere else for that matter), I'll point you in the general direction because I've only looked into it when it was still new.

put avsgrabber.ape and out_grab.dll in ..\winamp\plugins\avs\


Load a preset
Open to the editor
Add misc > "AVSgrabber 2.0 alpha" to the bottom of the list of effects and select it.
click browse and name your file
leave playback rate as it is
choose your output format (it may be that your divx codec won't work.. some just don't, just fiddle with it until you find a codec that works - or else try raw 32-bit mode and compress it later with some divx compression tool)
hit [Grab!] and hit it again to [stop] recording.


/sidenote:
it's possible that out_grab.dll must be placed in ..\winamp\plugins\ or c:\windows\system32\
I don't know either. Do some testing

That's about all the help I can give you.
