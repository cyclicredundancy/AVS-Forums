Well you've got further than a lot of noobs by not giving us all a seizure ;) good job for that. This is a good noob preset because i can see you're experimenting with movements. Try to experiment with some simple scopes next. Have a look around the forums for tutorials. The tips and tricks threads in the main forum would be a good place to start. Ateros primer, soon to be updated for the past six months.

Some tips: "i" is only a set variable in scopes. It doesn't do anything in movements unless you set a variable with the name "i". A unique tone tone sets the tone for things before it in the list so it doesn't do anything at the start. I suspect you want the screen to start orange so you want a render/clear screen to start.

Overall it's a very noob preet but it's watchable and not stupidly fast or bright. keep avsing and welcome to the forums :)
