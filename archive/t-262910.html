put your folder inside a temporary extra folder, i.e. create a new AVS folder (on your desktop perhaps).
Inside that folder, create the a new folder with your name, inside that folder a new folder with the name of your compilation. Copy your .avs files there, and your images and such to (your temporary) /avs

In PimpBot, select your temporary AVS folder and make it include subfolders.

When you're done, test your installer, then remove the temporary folder

If you've submitted it to winamp.com, there's been (or perhaps still are) some problems with the site, so it could take some weeks/months before it's up there..
