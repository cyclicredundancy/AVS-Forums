Here's one I used. for a star field.

per frame:
xs=17;

per pixel:
xs=(296*xs+123)%500;
x1=xs/500*2-2;
You could do something like this.
init:
counts=5;

per frame:
counts=if(equal(count,0),(296*xs+123)%500,counts);
count=if(equal(count,0),counts/500*range+minvalue,count-1);
or something like that.
