Hello all!

We've just released a new version of our APE plugin for AVS, iJenAPE. For those of you who don't know, this APE uses iJen to capture motion from your webcam and renders it inside the AVS.

It hasn't been published on winamp.com yet, but you can get a copy from http://www.ijen-ape.com/.

We may be good at making motion detection technology over here, unfortunately we suck at making AVS presets. So here's the challenge: Can you make a super sweet AVS preset using iJenAPE? We'd love to see it. :)

To use iJenAPE you'll need a USB webcam and drivers. It must support at least one kind of RGB output and a resolution of 320x240. You'll also need DirectX9.0a or greater.

Regards,
The SYFi Team.
