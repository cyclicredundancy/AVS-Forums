Moving Particle
Ok here's the first thing I could think of that I felt needed improving. Moving Particle.

We should be able to control more than just size, distance from centre etc.

Why not getting control over the the movement - as well as the random circling around the centre, why not stuff like going around the edges, or bouncing off stuff like a pingpong ball - or better still being able to programme its path of movement.

Why not also being able to control its shape, perhaps even being able to draw your own moving particle.

And also how about a few more on beat effects, such as on beat fade, on beat disappear, or on beat change colour?

I'm sure this one isn't too hard to alter for AVS version 3.0!
