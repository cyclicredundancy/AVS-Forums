Start avs, right click on window, select AVS editor, select 'settings' from that.

Check 'overlay mode', select the colour of your background image colour from the colour selector and away you go.

So if your powerpoint background image is black, select that in the colour picker, anything that is then black will be replaced by avs, if you text is white, that won't be affected, note everything that is black will also be replaced, so you might wanna pick an obscure colour for your background image, less chance of it conflicting with other stuff that way.
