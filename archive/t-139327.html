Unless a mod moves it first...

Anyway, sorry to say this but your website is pretty bad. A first visit should give a user a quick idea of what the website is about, content should be split into subjects across several pages and generally you should use less animations, clearer layout and better colors. Because of the information overload, I totally missed the link to your presets even though it was right under my nose.
And it's best not to put one of those "you are the n'th visitor" counter on your page (especially not at the top) because when you think about it, the only one interested in it is you. You're much better off using a discrete counter (like nedstat).

About your presets, it's clear you haven't done much AVS yet. Everyone is always impressed with the first presets they make, but the fact is, they're mostly created by the built-in effects, and your contribution to the preset is very small. Try creating more special and unique effects. You could take a look at other people's presets and dissect them to learn.
Of course I don't mean for you to stop AVSing, but just make sure you have something creative and unique when you post it on the internet. You don't have to publish everything you make... and if you only publish the best, then people will have a much better impression of your work.
