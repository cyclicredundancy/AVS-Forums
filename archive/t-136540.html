Visag - good luck with your first gig!

For project I'd reccommend a minimum 1000 ansi lumens projector. When you're buying look for features like through distance, svideo/av/monitor imputs, contrast ratio and bulb life.

Its probably worth your while hiring one to start off with but hell why not buy one outright. You can pick up pretty descent ones on ebay for a bit over a grand.

Also get yourself a video mixer - running two machines if definately worth your while and a video mixer gives you the effective music simulation that avs can't!

Trippy visuals are great things but! If it don't work with the music it just won't go down that special.

I'd reccommend rabid hamsters r2e plugin for a bit of fun also.

Then when you want to video mix - try something like robotfunks flowmotion :)

If you want to make the move to buy yourself a video mixer let me know. I can get a mx12 for around $650 (which is damn cheap).
