How to do it
Ok I was asked how I did AVS to live music. pretty simple, take an output from your board and put it into the line in on your sound card and make sure it's not muted in the volume control in windows. Then go into Winamp and add url "linein://" it will make a entry in your playlist called "Line Input" once you hit play it will start your input going through Winamp then just start AVS. Easy as PIE ;-b
