Optimizations
I optimized the Firework Preset a lot and put everything in 1 texer, using some loop()s :)
The particles are now drawn like this:

Point 0: particle #0 left eye
Point 1: particle #0 right eye
Point 2: particle #1 left eye
Point 3: particle #1 right eye
Point 4: particle #2 left eye
Point 5: particle #2 right eye
...
Point n*2-1: particle #n-1 left eye
Point n*2: particle #n-1 right eye

I could put the settings into the Texer, too, but I left them in the effect list for 2 reasons:
1. You can change the settings while the simulation is running.
2. You can edit the settings better in the (big) effect list edit box and don't have to worry about accidentally changing important code.
