Why did you make the circles from points when lines would do a much better and cheaper (in terms of FPS) job, especially when combined with a 'set render mode'?

Try using pi=2*acos(-1) in 'Init', ad q=1;asp=h/w; to the end of 'Per Frame' and use:

q=-q;
x=cos(pi*i)*.1+ax;
y=sin(q*i*pi)*.1+ay;
x=x*asp;

'Per Point'. Now you can make a great looking circle with 200 points or even less if combined with a 'set render mode'.
