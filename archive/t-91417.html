yes-it's quite simple. You can do it any number of ways. A shortcut key to toggle Random on/off is r while the AVS window is open & selected. To change the random settings, double-click the AVS window to open up the AVS editor. Click on Settings. Then select Presets & Hotkeys. It's on the bottom half of the screen.
