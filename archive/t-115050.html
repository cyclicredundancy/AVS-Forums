Fits to Trance/Goa, has got a Sunrise, quite fast
Hi,

if you like Trance music, full moon parties in Goa, the sun rise, and flying leaves, you should try my first AVS experiment.

It's quite fast (10 fps in 640*480 with 1 GHz Pentium) as I avoided all effect lists with there time consuming rendering. Nevertheless its cycle is quite long: Trans/Dynamic Movement shows three different phases controlled by one parameter. The background changes after 80 and 240 sec significantly. It will return to the first design after 500 sec in total (depending on the beat. btw: Does the beat detection works well? The CD cover says 140 bpm, AVS just finds 70?).

The objects (sun and leaves) are generated with Render/Superscope. It took hours to do the maths for the flying leaves. One problem was the limited space for the per-point-calculation. So don't expect 'speaking' names for the variables.

Just try it. If you don't like it, don't blame me, just delete it (as I did with all the slow presets).

CU in Goa
