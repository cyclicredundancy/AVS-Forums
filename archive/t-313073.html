Here is a quicky made one:

(Add a superscope and use this as the code.)

Init:
n=1000;

Frame:
asp=h/w;
c=0;

Point:
x=(i-.5)*asp;
y=if(c,0,1)-.5;
c=bnot(c);

col=getspec(0.1,.1,0);
red=col;green=col;blue=col;


Hope that helps.
