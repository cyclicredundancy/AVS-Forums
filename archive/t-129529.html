Here's my detailed comments:

A Mere Grid - A mere grid with lovely colors. Movement isn't anything spectacular and the edges definitely show, but doesn't detract from the preset too much.
Shine! - Even without a flowing DM this preset flows wonderfully. Love the colors and the shine.
The Core - Nice DM(I thought it was a morphing SSC until I looked at the code - amazing), but the preset as a whole doesn't do much. Great DM, though.
Shine - Oozic Remix - Different effect, but I liked the first one more. The Channel Shift in this one is a bit too obvious.
Comatose Gone Mad - Best effect I've ever seen using the colorfade/water effects.
Particle Cube - Excellent even without the particles. Really slow, though.
Particle Cube - Texture Cube Remix - Like this one more. Nice "texture" effect.
Swirling Mass - Definitely looks nice. Would be even nicer with some horizontal and vertical movements.
Oldskool Stuff - Nice oldskool stuff. Not my favorite, but has a nice effect to it.
Nuclear - Turns a bit too white(and I don't really think it looks like Paranoya's - maybe if you threw in a starfield and some weird SSC's:p ). Nice movement.
The Snowflakes - Not the most exciting preset, but I like the synching of the "wind".
Codeless Warping - Kinda interesting but not really.
Floating Lines - Interesting, but needs something else. Maybe a moving camera or something.
Alphacore - Interesting, smooth preset. Kinda mesmerizing.
Sea of Colors 2 - Very smooth, and the colors work well. Really like it with the kaleidoscope stuff.
Pillars of Thought - Looks awesome except for the lines going across the screen(you could probably just increase the camera distance a little to fix them).
Your Typical Oscilliscope - Really nice looking. Try adding some horizontal and vertical movement to make it a bit more interesting.
Randomly Walking - Very nice effects, and is interesting.
Polygons - Would be better if it slowly changed the number of sides, but that is difficult to code. Nothing amazing, but I do like the fill.
Liquefy - Colors get kinda weird looking sometimes, but it's a nice flow preset.
Dot Warpin' - Is very interesting to watch. Quick preset and looks nice.
Back To MP - Edges get a little nasty at times, but very nice beat responsive preset.
Containment - Weird movement with the MP's - I like it. Also a nice trail effect.
Interaction - Looks like the same movement of the particle in Containment, but in 3D. Like the flashing sides.
System - Very interesting with some cool fake 3D stuff.
Bass Spin - Don't like. The Bass Spin is one of the worst effects in AVS(IMO).
Zero Render - Don't really like the moving/shaking, but I do like the getspec movement.
Intro Pack 6 - Why is this at the end?:p "See you in Â±4 months??? So you will release the seventh pack sometime in between four months ago and four months from now?:p Also, not all of the beams are shooting off correctly(like the top right of the 'C').

Overall:4.0. Excellent pack. Cut down on the Channel Shift use though. Can't wait for the seventh pack.
