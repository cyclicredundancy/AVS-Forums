the music animation machine
this has nothing to do with avs, but i thought it'd be interesting to avs people.

the music animation machine is a midi player, the main attraction are the different modes of visualization. not customizable, but pretty fun to watch.

some crazy guy composed special midis that create the most awesome visualizations in the music animation machine, unfortunately the original midis are nowhere to be found. check it out though, you'll be amazed. specially the 'retro game medley' one.
