Good use of colors, the preset's ok if you don't know how to code your own scopes. Many of the built ins and APE's, (FyrewurX, roto-blitter, particle, etc.) can be made using the dynamic movements, super-scopes, and other Intimidating:eek: effects. I know it's hard, but try to slowly pull yourself away from using the built-ins. They are good for a beginning, but can't do everything you want.
Just keep at it and you'll get better. :)
