pimpbot appreciation
since the first release of pimpbot in january 2002, pimpbot has proven to be a convenient way to create installers tailor-made to install avs presets. i remember writing script to install presets manually, and i also remember bad scripted installers or lame winzip selfextractors being used to distribute presets.

as much as a gift pimpbot might have been to many of the artists from the inner scene, there's still a lot of badly packaged downloads available (especially on winamp.com). for multiple times i tried to speak to the responsible persons on this sites, including many forums moderators. still a recommendation in the download section or a news article presenting pimpbot to the masses of artists on this site has never been available.

the idea was to collect a few presets from the artists of the community in order to release a pimpbot appreciation pack. i've got to admit that the main drive that made me write pimpbot where the many releases i was (and am) doing myself, and i never minded sharing the tool (and later its source-code). if you appreciated pimpbot in the past, you can now show your appreciation by contributing to the pack i got in mind.

submissions go to <my nick on this board> at visbot.net

no need to rush things, lets have late august in mind as a lose deadline.

thanks for your support!
