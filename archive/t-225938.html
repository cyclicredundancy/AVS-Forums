the way overlay works is by replacing a colour in the 'final screen render' with the visualisation. alpha blending changes the colour of the desktop from the overlay color hence breaking it.

this isnt a bug. its how overlay mode works unfortunately. its also non-trivial to work around, so i wouldnt expect a fix ever.
