Good presets. Here are some tips.
01-Blob- Not a big fan of dot fountain, but good job using it. A SSC would be a little nicer because of color changing. Try fixing the second DM. Manipulation of R has to be done in whole number increments or else you get that horizontal line.
02-Colors- Good preset, but a little repetitive in the movements. Also, it turns completely grey sometimes.
03-Conveyor Belt- Doesn't look much like a conveyor belt(white??) and isn't very sophisticated, but good job for your first 3-D DM.
04-Curvy 3D- Makes me think of bending space-time... Very good job with transition with curves.
05-Emotions in Chalk- What kind of chalk does your sister use?:p Looks a little grainy, but besides that it's a good preset. Moving particle doesn't seem to do much and the blocky partial out can go too.
06-Into the fire- The background doesn't really fit with the foreground. Both parts are nice though.
07-Kaleidoscope tiling- Somewhat repetitive, but I like the effect. Make it change a little more and it will be a really good preset.
08-More colors- Kinda boring. Add a kaleidoscopic effect to it and I think it might turn out well. Also, colorcode the superscopes, the colors get kinda repetitive.
09-Peyote- Not very fast (16FPS windowed 320x200 on my PIII 1GHz.) but it is music responsive. The many max blends are causing the colors to interfere with eachother and they also suck framerates.
10-Road- The grass looks weird off in the distance and isn't moving at the same speed as the road. Also, the road needs to turn some (either that or take out the grass and make it look like montana:) )
11-Seashell- Nice for a 2-D seashell. Btw, any preset that you use a time variable in uses a custom counter(i.e. t).
12-Techno Rings- Good preset. It might look better if you make it rotate some more.
13-Televised- Decent cube DM. Don't really like the fireworks/water combo. Also, try moving the camera around some.
14-Vertigo- Fix the comment, it says this is Blob. Good preset though. A little on the repetitive side.
15-<>- This is the worst preset in the pack:) .
16-Window on my world- Don't really like the white square. It sticks out like a sore thumb. The xor effect is alright though.
17-NoName- The SSC is feeling a bit lonely(he's in a white room) and sad(he doesn't have a name). Give him some friends and a name.
18-Bloom- Looks a lot like something I was working on the other day. I like it.

Overall rating - 4.2. Very good job. Fix some stuff in it. Some of the more basic ones could use a complete remix.
