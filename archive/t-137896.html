...
x2=x2*ct-z2*st;
z2=-x2*st-z2*ct;

you're altering x2, a term used in the z2 statment, before you get to the z2 statement. to get it to work properly use different variable names, or a storage variable:
x3=x2*ct-z2*st;
z2=-x2*st-z2*ct;
or
x2a=x2; x2=x2*ct-z2*st;
z2=-x2a*st-z2*ct;
