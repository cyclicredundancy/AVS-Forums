Alpha blending only works in windowed mode, because overlay is a hardware 'hack'. In overlay mode, a certain color (a key) is used for your desktop color, which is then replaced by the overlayed image by your videocard before the signal is sent to your monitor.

To see how it works, take screenshot with overlay mode on, and look at it with overlay mode off. You will only see a solid desktop.
