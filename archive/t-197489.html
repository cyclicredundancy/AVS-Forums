Is it possible to change the location of my avs presets (and more importantly the media content.)as I want them to sit on my video drive?

UnConeD
26th October 2004 14:00 GMT

You can't change the location of the presets directly, but there is a sort of roundabout way: change the location of the Winamp visualisation plugin directory. You can do this in Winamp's preferences -> plug-ins.

Move vis_avs.dll to the newly created directory, make an AVS subdirectory in there and put all your AVS stuff (presets, pictures, APEs) in there.
