UUUUUUUNGH! Here ya go :)

authority - Nice design and that wiggly dm ain't that bad, but the flashing background gets on the nerves after a while. Also the beat detection could be a little better, now it's kinda hard to tell when it reacts to beat...

bad influences - Nice superscopes and the background looks cool, though it could stick a bit more out.

cream dream - Maybe a little too minimalistic on the pattern, but otherwise looks pretty neat-o.

cubesex - Nice superscopes and the starfield looks great. But the cubes themself don't look 3D since they sometimes get squeezed :igor:

freedom - Nice simple preset and beat responsive. Though you could fix the "wrap" lines, try example to use a 1x1 grid size in the dm. Works for me.

go away bastards! - Looks nice, but why the hell you are using a dm with r=-r*3; ? You know you should use a regular movement, because you are not making it dynamic and a movement is more accurate.

pointless - Nice scope, the background pattern also looks cool. I really love the effect with the convolution filter.

there still is hope - Another 3D preset, not much to say. The scope reminds me of JDm's "Whitespace". The starfield doesn't look so good in this one, because there is a big glob of stars in the middle.

universal fish - Like the idea, pretty beat responsive and the scope is sound responsive. Only thing this could need is that the starfield could be a bit brighter since it's kinda hard to see it.

wild west - Done, Done & Done before. Still i like this one, because of the simple pattern and it flies nicely.



Overall a good B&W pack. I see lot of improving in here. Keep up the good work :up:
