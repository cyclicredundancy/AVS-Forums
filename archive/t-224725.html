As far as I know, it was never possible to open AVS files that way. I might be wrong, though...

You can load an AVS file with "Preset / Load" in the AVS editor menu. That will work.

Or, better yet, you can move the file into your "WinAmp/Plugins/avs" directory where it'll be accessible through the pull-down menu, and also the "Next" and "Prev" and "Random" buttons.
