Anyways, Strider:
The process is easy.
-Go to http://www.winamp.com
-Click on the components tab
-Click on the "Submit a Component" link - it's right up there in the top right of the screen.
-You will need to make a winamp user account first - doing this shouldn't be hard
-Once you have an account go back to the "Submit a Component" page, and sign in with your username.
-Click "Publish a New Skin or Component" on the right of the screen.
-Follow the instructions.

Note: You can only upload an executable, so you have a couple of options on what to do:
1. Learn NSIS and make your own nifty installer script.
2. Use someone else's installer script - several AVS people have nice ones - you will just have to make some slight modifications.
3. This is the easiest one, but also the least professional - just use winamp to create a self-extracting archive.

Hope this helps. If not, just axe more questions.
