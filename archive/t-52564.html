Pres "R" for turn OFF/ON random chnage
Pres "Y" for backward of AVS preset
Pres "U" for forward of AVS preset
Pres Ctrl+(F1 to F12, 1 to 0, Shift+1 to Shift+0) to save presets in memory
Pres F1 to F12, 1 to 0, Shift+1 to Shift+0 to load from memory
