The thing to remember is,not every reviewer knows every component/skin/preset that is published in the world, so it's very easy to publish something that is a direct rip off of someone elses work, I've done it myself on a number of occasions.

If you see something that you know is a rip, and can back that up, head to the rip reporting forum and shout up.
http://forums.winamp.com/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=119

Rips won't be dealt with on a forum by forum basis, thats what the rip reporting forum is for, to keep it all in one neat place that the reviewers can check without having to look through individual forums.

Rest assured it will be dealt with if there is sufficient evidence to remove it.

In answer to your other question, yes reviews can and do deny packs/plugs/skins from being published, but again it goes back to point one, they would have to know each and every single component that has ever been released, and that is a tall order for anyon
