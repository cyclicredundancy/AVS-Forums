Stuffs - First AVS pack
This is just a random collection of presets ive made over the last year or so for my own enjoyment. Decided to release them so other people can take a look if they so desire. Any feedback on how to make them better and/or adoring praise is welcome!

Silhouette - One of my favorites of the pack
Converge - Inspired by the launch video for the 2009 NCYC
Petals o' Sound - Just something i made while experimenting, kinda looks like a flower.
Smooth Wave - Simple yet elegant waveform
MultiWave - Another waveform, nothing to flash
Inverted MultiWave - Read the name
Lines - Basically the same as Converge but with some different colours and stuff
3D Blobs - my first dablings in 3D presets, nothing to special
