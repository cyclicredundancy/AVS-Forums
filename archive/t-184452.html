ry=atan2(-ox,-oz);
rx=atan2(oy,sqrt(sqr(oz)+sqr(ox)));
rz=0;

this is for zxy rotated superscopes for zyx just swap appropriate vars.

it comes from pythagoras theorem and some trig, draw a 3d diagram to see how it works.
