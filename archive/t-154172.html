I see you like the onbeat sizechange for moving particles. I remember when I first started out in AVS how amazed at the simplicity of creating such cool graphics!

Well, as you grow, you realize where you were a little while ago was just a temporary position---and soon you start trying new stuff with AVS and experimenting.

Actually, this preset reminded me sorta of the moon phases or a Ms Pacman game. It's a great start! Continue to tinker in AVS. There are several guides, primers, and websites available to aid you in understanding Winamp Advanced Visualization Studio. Note: Do not confuse AVS (adult verification system) with Winamp Advanced Visualization Studio when using a search engine. You want Advanced Visualization Studio. Plus if you do a forum search you should be able to find the threads. AVS primer, AVS guide, or several other terms should work.
