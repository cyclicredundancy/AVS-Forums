btw...if this is a remix, then you should be more clear in the title. Take the original name, then add on to it
for example, if you were to remix UnConeD's Jello Cube, you would take the original name

UnConeD - VI - 03 - Jello Cube

then add on my remix name

UnConeD - VI - 03 - Jello Cube - accentmatt rainbow RMX

Then, include the original comment at the very top. Otherwise, people will think you are just a ripper.
